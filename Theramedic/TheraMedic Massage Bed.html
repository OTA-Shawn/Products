  • The Theramedic Bed includes a dash of luxury features, such as a remote controller,
    air compression massages, and heating zones.

    720° Therapy: complete body of manual functions that lasts for 30 minutes.
    360° Therapy: the same complete body of manual functions that lasts for 20 minutes.

    Other therapy modes include:
    Neck & Waist Discomfort, Physical & Mental Fatigue, Muscle Ache, Sleep Disorder, Stomach Discomfort, Postpartum Recovery, Overweight Problem, Elderly Health, Osteoporosis Prevention

    Acoustic Vibration is a manual mode that engages the full body vibration plate
    to create lateral vibrations for 10 minutes.

    The Hi-Speed Sieve is a manual mode that engages the full body vibration plate,
    creating longitudinal vibrations for 10 minutes.

    Gradient Pressure is a manual mode that includes a sequence of air compression massages in 25 minutes.
    This mode utilizes air bags located along the neck, lumbar, calves, and feet.

    While in a reclincation of 10”, Inversion Traction engages a sequence of air compression massages in 10 minutes.

    The TheraMedic Bed offers heating zones along the feet and lumbar areas, helping release muscle tensions.
    You have the option of turning it on with any of the massage modes.

    The TheraMedic Bed allows you to adapt the massage to your preferences using the remote controller,
    which provides 11 Therapy modes and 4 Manual modes.

    Create an ambience by playing the TheraMedic Bed’s preprogrammed instrumental music
    and white noises of water.

    The TheraMedic Bed’s convenient remote holder provides easy access and convenient location,
    making it hassle-free for the user.

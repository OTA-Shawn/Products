Otamic Signature is a 3D adjustable massage chair with an advanced SL-track rolling system. The Otamic Signature massage chair comes furnished with a computer body scan, full body airbag massage, Zero Gravity with 21 auto massage programs, and 6 massage styles.

The Otamic Signature includes advanced technology which is an intelligent voice recognition. Voice or speech recognition is the ability of devices to record spoken words or commands by the user. The Otamic Signature has a tiny microphone around the Armrest Shortcut area which recognizes 15 different phrases that the user can manage the massage modes by speaking.

The SL-Track rollers will contour the natural curvature of your spine as well as massaging the neck to the glutes.

Otamic Signature is an Advanced 3D massage chair that is equipped with 5 levels of 3D adjustment, 5 of speed, 5 levels of strength, 5 levels of airbag intensity, and 5 levels of width adjustment. Advanced 3D lets you adjust the speed and width of 3D rollers at any time in a massage, whether you are in auto or manual mode.

The Otamic Signature will perform a body scan to map the key areas of the neck and back to ensure the optimal amount of pressure applied during the massage. The scanning technology will detect your shoulders height, which can be re-calibrated once the scan is complete.

The Otamic Signature is designed with efficiency in mind, and that includes the entire airbag system in the chair. The Otamic Signature offers a total of 32 airbags in the massage chair. This also increases the longevity and reliability of the chair.

The Otamic Signature is equipped with Zero Gravity reclining, giving the user a weightless feeling during the massage and the amount of pressure they would like in their massage. This gives the user an option on how far back they want their chair to recline automatically, or you can adjust the chair manually with the remote. 3 Reclining Stages (Initial Step, Comfort Step, Deep Step).

The Otamic Signature slides forwards to create more space saving from the wall to allow the chair to be within 2 inches away from the wall. Maximizing living space in your home.

The Otamic Signature has 21 extensive auto program features. Automatic programs are combinations of different massage styles that focuses in on different areas of the user's body.

The Otamic Signature has heating pads located on the Lumbar area. It offers heat therapy which is a great compliment to the roller and airbag massage. Heating the body's temperature is known to increase blood circulation and loosen tense muscles.

The foot massage rollers that spin along the arch of the feet is complimented by airbag massage. Calf rollers comes standard with the Otamic Signature for a fuller feet and leg massage. As the airbags inflate the feet are pushed down on the spinning rollers increasing the intensity.

Calf Kneading feature is located in the center console of the footrest. The air massagers will rotate in a circular motion to add a new dimension to the calf massage.

The footrest can be extended up to 6.5" to ensure the optimal leg length for your height. The foot component will extend outwards and then retract inwards. When adjusting the footrest, the user may apply pressure on the footrest to stop at the desired position.

Located near the Wireless Charging pad, the user is able to adjust the 3D level of the track as well as powering on/off the massage chair. Wireless Charging Pad is available standard in the Otamic Signature massage chair. Convenient way to charge a compatible.

The Otamic Signature is equipped with Bluetooth technology that reduces the surrounding noise, enhancing your listening experience.

Illuminate the room with Chromo Therapy Lighting. The soothing blue light enhances your experience and heightens your sense of relaxation.

    The Titan Pro Commander massage chair features an L-Track system with 3D rollers, zero gravity, 6 auto programs, and easy to use remote for the most relaxing massage experience. Packed with unique elbow pads, this chair is ready to give the best user experience. It also offers a unique remote pocket.

    3D Massage

    The next generation of 3D massage rollers are designed for  outstanding range and flexibility. The 3D technology allows the user to control the length of rollers that extend out from the backrest.

    L-Track Massage

    As the roller system massages the neck area, it continues through to the lower back and then proceeds to massage the glutes and upper hamstrings.

    Full Body Compression Masssage

    There are multiple airbags located throughout the massage chair to offer a full body massage experience. The Commander offers a total of 12 airbags in the massage chair. Airbags are located at the shoulders, arms, feet, and the calf areas. This compression massage helps to continue increasing blood circulation throughout the body to relieve pain and tension.

    Specialized Foot Massage

    There are spinning reflexology rollers located at the bottom of the footrest providing a soothing kneading massage. When the rollers spin, they will stimulate the acupuncture points.

    Three Stage Zero Gravity Reclining

    Inspired by NASA technology, the Zero gravity position is the perfect position to enjoy a massage, aligning your back relative to your thigh at an optimal level of comfort. When reclined into the zero -gravity position, the complete weight of your back is virtually supported by the backrest which maximizes the intensity of the massage.

    Extendable Footrest

    Titan Pro Commander has an extendable footrest which can be extended up to 6.5 Inches.

    5 Auto Massage Programs

    The Titan Pro Commander has 5 unique pre-programmed massage functions. Each offering different combination of massage styles and focused area.

    4 Massage Styles

    The Titan Pro Commander massage chair comes with manual mode so you can pinpoint a desired massage for a specific area on your body. Commander can perform Kneading, Tapping, Tapping and Kneading and Shiatsu. You can either pinpoint a specific spot on your back for the massage or massage by sections of the body.

    Multi-language Interface

    You can meet four kinds of language options to choose. There is English, Traditional Chinese, Simplified Chinese and Korean.

    Surround Sound Bluetooth Speakers

    The Titan Pro Commander is equipped with Bluetooth technology that allows the user to listen to their favorite music and phone calls on high quality speakers in the headrest.

    Easy to Use Remote

    The Titan Pro Commander remote has an easy view screen displaying the function the chair has activated. On the remote it allows you to select the massage type, intensity, speed, and location. You can also operate the functions independently in the Manual Mode.

    Lumbar Heat Therapy

    The Titan Pro Commander has 2 heating pads located on the back area, which enhances the effect of the massage.

    Elbow and Neck Cushion

    The Titan Pro Commander has cushions located on the elbow and neck area, that makes you enjoy more comfortable massage.

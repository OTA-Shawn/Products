Voice Recognition
The Elite includes advanced technology which is an intelligent voice recognition. Voice or speech recognition is the ability of devices to record spoken words or commands by the user. The Titan Elite 3D has a tiny microphone around the Armrest Shortcut area which recognizes 15 different phrases that the user can manage the massage modes by speaking.
Advanced 3D Technology
The Titan Elite 3D massage chair has 3D technology that allows for 5 levels of massage roller adjustment, including depth, speed, and width. The depth adjustment can be made at any point during the massage, even in auto or manual mode. There are also 5 levels of back massage intensity available.
Auto Body Scan Technology
The Elite will perform a body scan to map the key areas of the neck and back to ensure the optimal amount of pressure applied during the massage. The scanning technology will detect your shoulders height, which can be re-calibrated once the scan is complete.
SL-Track Massage
The Elite also comes standard with the latest massage rolling track, which massages from your neck, all the way down to your glutes and upper hamstrings. This is called an "SL" rolling track, and it works with the computer body scan at the beginning of every session to give every user a massage that fits their body.
3 Steps of Zero Gravity
The Titan Elite 3D is equipped with 3 steps of Zero Gravity, giving the user a weightless feeling during the massage. This gives the user an option on how far back they want their chair to recline automatically, or you can adjust the chair manually with the remote. Different steps of Zero Gravity offers various types including Initial Step, Comfort Step and Deep Step.
Space Saving Technology
The Elite slides forwards to create more space saving from the wall to allow the chair to be within 2 inches away from the wall. Maximizing living space in your home.
30 Full Body Airbag Massage
The Elite is designed with efficiency in mind, and that includes the entire airbag system in the chair. The Titan Elite 3D offers a total of 30 airbags in the massage chair. The stream-lined design uses less airbags, while still giving a full-body air compression massage. This also increases the longevity and reliability of the chair.
Heating on Lumbar
The Elite has heating pads located on the Lumbar area. It offers the heat therapy which is a great compliment to the roller and airbag massage. Heating the body's temperature is known to increase blood circulation and loosen tense muscles.

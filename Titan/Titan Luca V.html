The Titan Luca V Massage Chair is equipped with the latest features at an credible value! The Luca V is an SL-Track chair with many classic design elements such as a extending legrest, foot rollers, space saving (4" from the wall) and bluetooth speakers. The Titan Luca V has an easy to use remote allowing the user to customize their massage using a large variety of features and options. Enjoy the luxuries of your private massage in the comfort of your own home knowing its backed by one of the most trusted brands in the industry.

Advanced L-Track Massage

As the roller system massages into the neck area, it continue through to the lower back and then proceeds to massage the glutes to the hamstrings. The SL-Track system will contour the curves on the back to provide consistent pressure from top to bottom.

Full Body Compression Massage

There are multiple airbags located throughout the massage chair to offer a full body massage experience. The Luca V offers a total of 16 airbags in the massage chair. Airbags are selectively located at the shoulders, arms, feet, and the calf areas. This compression massage promotes blood circulation throughout the body. The latest airbag massage technology has reduced the airbag count but yet increasing the surface area with far greater dependability.

Advanced Foot Rollers

There are spinning reflexology rollers located at the bottom of the footrest providing a soothing kneading massage. When the rollers spin, they will stimulate the acupuncture points. The airbags inflate to hold your legs in place for a more accurate and deeper massage.

Lumbar Heat Therapy

The Titan Luca V has 2 heating pads located in the lumbar area. The heat therapy helps to loosen muscle which allow the back rollers to apply a more effective massage. The temperature of the heat is mild using infrared technolgy with a minimal amount of power consumption.

Space Saving Technology

The Titan Luca V only needs 4 inches from the backrest to the wall, taking much less space than the traditional massage chair. As you recline the massage chair, the chair slides forward, which means less space is needed, saving up to 2 feet of space.

Surround Sound Bluetooth Speakers

The Titan Luca V is equipped with Bluetooth technology that allows you to listen to your favorite music and phone calls on high quality speakers located in the headrest.

Extendable Footrest

The footrest can extend up to 7.3 inches to accommodate a large variety of heights up to 6'3".

4 Auto Massages

Luca V is designed with 4 sets of auto massage programs each unique that will concentrate on certain massage style.

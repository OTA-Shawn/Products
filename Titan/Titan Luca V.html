The Titan Luca V is an advanced 2D massage chair with Ergonomic SL-track rolling system. The chair comes equipped with a computer body scan system, compression massage with 18 airbags, heating on the lumbar, calf and foot roller massage with 8 automatic massage programs.
Advanced L-Track Massage
As the roller system massages into the neck area, it continue through to the lower back and then proceeds to massage the glutes to the hamstrings. The SL-Track system will contour the curves on the back to provide consistent pressure from top to bottom.
Zero Gravity
The Luca V comes with zero gravity, giving the user a weightless feeling during the massage. Zero Gravity gives the user an option on how far back they want their chair to recline automatically, or you can adjust the chair manually in the remote.

The Titan Omega 3D is a 3D massage chair that offers a long SL-track rolling system. Unlike many other 3D Massage chairs, the Omega 3D is exclusively engineered with the latest massaging motors, making it one of the smoothest, quietest, and one of the most reliable massage experiences offered. The chair comes equipped with3 adjustable calf roller and advanced foot roller and air massage.  The Omega 3D also is equipped the latest generation of airbag massage systems, that covers the major key areas of the body with high efficiency!

The Omega 3D comes equipped with a 3 levels of 3D adjustable massage rolling system. The 3D technology gives you control over the overall intensity of the massage, by moving the rollers in and out from the backrest. Unlike most 3D Massage chairs in the market, the chair lets you adjust the 3D Roller depth at any point in the massage, whether you are in an auto mode, or a manual mode. On top of the depth adjustment, there are 4 levels of speed adjustment, 3 levels of strength adjustment and 3 levels of width adjustment.

The Omega 3D will perform a body scan to map the key areas of the neck and back to ensure the optimal amount of pressure applied during the massage. The scanning technology will detect your shoulders height, which can be re-calibrated once the scan is complete.

The Titan Omega 3D comes standard with Titan’s latest massage rolling track, which massages from your neck, all the way down to your glutes and upper hamstrings. This is called an “SL” rolling track, and it works with the computer body scan at the beginning of every session to give every user a massage that fits their body.

This Chair comes equipped with 8 different preset programs including: Energy, Relax, Wake Up, Stretch, Swings, Sport, Recover and Intense. These auto programs combine the 6 different massage techniques available in the chair. These 6 massage styles are: Kneading, Tapping, Knead & Tap, Rolling, Shiatsu and Clapping.

The Omega 3D was designed with efficiency in mind, and that includes the entire airbag system in the chair. The stream-lined design uses less airbags, while still giving a full-body air compression massage. This also increases the longevity and reliability of the chair. There are 28 airbags located throughout the massage chair to offer a full body massage experience. With the 28 airbags it covers more body surface than its previous generations while applying a more consistent pressure throughout the chair.

The Omega 3D is equipped with 2 stages of zero gravity, giving the user a weightless feeling during the massage. This gives the user an option on how far back they want their chair to recline automatically, or you can adjust the chair manually with the remote.

Other than the remote control, The Titan Omega 3D has a convenient side controller on the right side of the side panel. This controller has various key features. It is very useful especially when the user wants immediate stop of massage or change the sitting position. Plus, it has USB port, so the user can charge their cell phone or tablet while having great massage experience.

Along with the Zero-Gravity recline, the Omega 3D comes standard with space-saving technology, that allows you to place the chair within 1 inch of the wall, even while fully-reclined. The chair will move out first and then up while moving into Zero-Gravity.

Located in the back portion of the calf massager are spinning rollers with 3 nodes that provides a smooth kneading style massage. On the bottom of the foot massager rollers that spin along the arch of the feet which is complimented by airbag massage. As the airbags inflate the feet are pushed down on the spinning rollers increasing the intensity.

Titan Omega 3D has an  Extendable Footrest which can be extended up to 6 Inches allowing to accommodate various heights.

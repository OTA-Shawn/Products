3D MASSAGE MECHANISM
The Titan Grande XL features a next-generation robotic 3D massage system. This innovative technology allows the massage heads to move up, down, side-to-side, and in and out from the backrest, enabling precise targeting of specific areas along the back.
INTELLIGENT VOICE CONTROL
The Grande XL's intelligent voice control feature lets you easily manage your massage experience hands-free. Simply use voice commands to adjust settings and activate specific massage programs, making relaxation more seamless and intuitive.
AUTOMATIC BODY SCAN
The Grande XL’s automatic body scan feature customizes each massage by detecting your body shape and contours. This smart technology ensures that the massage heads adjust perfectly to target your unique pressure points for a personalized, effective experience.
SPACE-SAVING TECHNOLOGY
The space-saving design enhances room efficiency by allowing the chair to slide forward as it reclines, providing full reclining capability with just 9 inches of clearance from the wall.
3-STAGE ZERO GRAVITY
The Titan Grande XL, inspired by NASA technology, features Zero Gravity positioning that raises your legs to heart level. This position evenly distributes your weight, reducing back pressure and improving leg circulation. It also allows the massage rollers to apply uniform pressure, providing a balanced and consistent massage from shoulders to lower back.
SL-TRACK SYSTEM
Built from precision laser-crafted steel, the Titan Grande XL’s SL-track technology allows the massage rollers to glide smoothly from your head to your hamstrings, delivering a thorough full-body massage.

INTELLIGENT VOICE CONTROL
The Titan Advent 4D features Intelligent Voice Control, which lets you adjust settings hands-free. Simply speak commands, and your chair responds instantly for a seamless, personalized massage experience.

AUTOMATIC BODY SCAN
Automatic body scan technology analyzes your body shape and contours to customize the massage experience. This ensures precise roller positioning and optimal comfort for maximum relaxation and effectiveness.
SPACE-SAVING TECHNOLOGY
Using Space-Saving Technology, the Titan Advent 4D can recline with 2” of space from the wall , sliding forward as it leans back. Perfect for small spaces, it maximizes comfort without taking up extra room.
3-STAGE ZERO GRAVITY RECLINING
Inspired by NASA technology, the Titan Advent 4D’s Zero Gravity Recline elevates your legs to heart level, reducing pressure on the spine and enhancing relaxation. This position promotes better circulation and a weightless, stress-free massage experience.
SL-TRACK SYSTEM DESIGN
The Titan Advent 4D offers SL-Track System that follows the natural curve of your spine, extending from the neck to the hamstrings. This ensures deep, continuous relief by maximizing coverage and enhancing the massage experience.

Measuring Guide
  • Calculate the doorway width by subtracting 1 inch from the smallest inner width of the door frame. (1/2 inch from each side)

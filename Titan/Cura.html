The Cura 4D’s 32-cell air compression massage system uses strategically placed air chambers to gently squeeze and release key areas of the body, mimicking natural hand movements. back & feet
HEATING therapy
The Cura 4D’s calf heating therapy delivers gentle, targeted warmth to help soothe tired lower back and feet and ease built-up muscle tension. By promoting better blood flow and deep relaxation, it enhances recovery and adds an extra layer of comfort during your massage experience.

Measuring Guide
  • Calculate the doorway width by subtracting 2 inches from the smallest inner width of the door frame. (1 / 2 inch from each side)
  • Please use a standard measuring tape to measure the doorway.
  • If the door cannot be fully opened, open it as much as possible and measure the width from the edge of the door hinge.
  • If there is a rounded curve on the wall, calculate the inner width based on the most protruding point of the wall.

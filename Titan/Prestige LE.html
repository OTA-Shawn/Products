Prestige LE is engineered to deliver a refined full-body massage experience through intelligent body scanning, advanced 3D massage technology, and comprehensive air compression. Featuring an ergonomic SL-Track system, Zero Gravity reclining, lumbar heating therapy, and 48-cell air massage, it combines advanced functionality with everyday comfort and relaxation.
Body Scan
System
The Titan Prestige LE will perform a body scan to map the key areas of the neck and back to ensure the optimal amount of pressure applied during the massage. The scanning technology will detect your shoulders height, which can be re-calibrated once the scan is complete.
Advanced
3D Massage
The next generation 3D massage rollers deliver enhanced range and flexibility. Users can easily adjust the roller extension for a more customized massage experience.
zero gravity reclining
The Prestige LE comes with zero gravity, giving the user a weightless feeling during the massage. Zero Gravity gives the user an option on how far back they want their chair to recline automatically, or you can adjust the chair manually in the remote.
SL-Track
SYSTEM
The extended SL track merges S and L shapes to follow your spine’s natural curve, reaching from neck to upper glutes for broader coverage and quicker recovery.
Space-saving
TEchnology
Engineered for space efficiency, the chair’s advanced technology lets it slide forward as it reclines, enabling a full recline even when positioned just 1 inches from the wall.
38-Cell
Full Body
Air massage
With 38 air cells placed across the shoulders, armrests, back, calves, and feet, the Prestige LE delivers full-body air compression. Specially designed calf support bumpers enhance contact around the calves, helping air pressure feel smoother, more precise, and more balanced.
Lumbar
Heat Therapy
The Prestige LE features lumbar heating pads designed to deliver soothing warmth to the lower back. This gentle heat complements the roller and airbag massage, helping promote circulation while easing tension for a more relaxing massage experience.
*The heating pads provide warmth in the range of approximately 75 - 83°F

MINIMUM DOORWAY
Measuring Guide
  • Calculate the doorway width by subtracting 2 inches from the smallest inner width of the door frame. (1 / 2 inch from each side)
  • Please use a standard measuring tape to measure the doorway.
  • If the door cannot be fully opened, open it as much as possible and measure the width from the edge of the door hinge.
  • If there is a rounded curve on the wall, calculate the inner width based on the most protruding point of the wall.

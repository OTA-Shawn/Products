Titan TP-Cosmo is a 2D massage chair with an advanced ergonomic SL-track rolling system. Unlike other 2D massage chairs, the TP-Cosmo comes with 12 different auto massage programs, 3 modes of pneumatic massage function, and selection of three different air pressure parts. Experience the most advanced 2D massage chair with the latest massage features including Voice Activation.
As Titan Osaki’s second chair made with voice control built-in, the Titan TP-Cosmo is ahead of most chairs on the market. To activate, just say “Hi Alice”, and you’ll have access to 17 different commands.
The Titan TP-Cosmo also comes standard with Titan’s latest massage rolling track, which massages from your neck, all the way down to your glutes and upper hamstrings. This is called an “SL” rolling track, and it works with the computer body scan at the beginning of every session to give every user a massage that fits their body.

Air Massage

The Titan TP-Cosmo is designed with long-term reliability in mind, and that includes the entire airbag system in the chair. There are 16 airbags in total located at shoulders, arms, calf areas and foot areas.

Heating

TP-Cosmo has heating pads located in the Lumbar area. It offers heat therapy which is a great compliment to the roller and airbag massage. Heating the body’s temperature is known to increase blood circulation and loosen tense muscles.
Side panel controller is designed that the user can easily control the chair without using a remote controller. The user can adjust calf lift, movement of upright and down position, Zero Gravity position, an automatic mode and power button.
Titan TP-Cosmo is equipped with Bluetooth technology that allows the user to listen to their favorite music and phone calls on high quality speakers in the headrest wirelessly.
This chair comes equipped with 8 different preset programs including comfort. Relax, stretch, shoulder & neck, back & waist, and more.
  • Comfort: The most comfortable massage experience.
  • Relax: Easy, soft, and comfortable massage and relieve body fatigue.
  • Stretch: The stretch massage uses the airbags to hold your body in place while the backrest reclines and stretches out your body.
  • Shoulder & Back: It focuses on shoulder and neck massage to relieve fatigue.
  • Back & Waist: It focuses on backrest and waist areas.
  • More: 7 different massage modes are included in the “more” section. You can find a full body massage, chiropractic massage, fatigue recovery, meridians massage, glutes massage, sleep massage and all pressure.
The Cosmo is equipped with 3 stages of zero gravity, giving the user a weightless feeling during the massage. This gives the user an option on how far back they want their chair to recline automatically, or you can adjust the chair manually with the remote.
Along with the Zero Gravity recline, the Cosmo comes standard with space-saving technology, which allows you to place the chair within 2 inches from the wall, even while fully reclined. The chair will move forward while moving into Zero-Gravity reducing wasted space.
The Cosmo has an Automatic Extendable Footrest which can be extended up to 6.5 inches, allowing it to accommodate various heights. When initiating the massage, the leg-rest can be extended completely out and then retracted.

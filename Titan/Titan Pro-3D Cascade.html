The Titan Cascade 3D is the latest edition to Titan’s ever-growing roster of high quality chairs. This sleek modern chair comes available in three colors (taupe, brown, and black), and includes a number of luxurious features. From an automated body scan and 6 automatic massages, to voice control and fatigue detection, the Cascade has it all! Treat yourself to a life of luxury with the brand new Titan Cascade 3D.

Intelligent Voice Control

The Cascade includes intelligent voice recognition, allowing the user to control the chair through 17 different voice commands. To initiate Voice Control, simply say "Hi Alice" or "Hey Alice”.

Intelligent Heart Rate Monitor

The Cascade comes equipped with an Automated Heart Rate Monitor. Simply rest your finger on the enhanced scanner to begin monitoring your heart rate in order to provide the best massage for your specific situation. This device SHOULD NOT be used as a replacement for certified medical equipment.

3D Massage Mechanism

The Titan Cascade 3D comes equipped with 5 levels of 3D Massage Roller Adjustment in addition to Massage Range & Width. The 3D technology allows you to control the overall intensity of the massage by managing the depth of the back rollers. Unlike most 3D Massage chairs on the market, the Cascade lets you adjust the 3D roller depth at any point in the massage, whether you're in an auto mode or a manual mode. On top of the depth adjustment, there are 5 levels of speed, area and width adjustment available, and 5 levels of back massage intensity.

Automatic Body Scan

The Cascade is designed with the function of automatic body detection and micro-adjustment of shoulder position. By automatically detecting the curve of your spine and specific body measurements, the chair makes every massage more humanized and scientific. The Titan Cascade 3D will perform a body scan to map the key areas of the neck and back to ensure the optimal amount of pressure applied during the massage. The scanning technology will detect your shoulders height, which can be re-calibrated once the scan is complete.

SL-Track Massage

Additionally, the Cascade comes standard with Titan's latest massage rolling track, which massages from your neck, all the way down to your glutes and upper hamstrings. This is called an "SL" rolling track, and it works with the automated body scan at the beginning of every session to give every user a massage that fits their body.

30 Air Cell Full Body Air Massage

The Cascade is designed with efficiency in mind, and that includes the entire airbag system in the chair. The Titan Cascade 3D offers a total of 30 air cells and 5 levels for adjustment in the massage chair. The stream-lined design uses less air cells, while still giving a full-body air compression massage, therefore increasing the longevity and reliability of the chair.

2 Stage Zero Gravity Reclining

The Titan Cascade 3D is equipped with 2 steps of Zero Gravity, giving the user a weightless feeling during the massage. This gives the user an option on how far back they want their chair to recline automatically, or you can adjust the chair manually with the remote.

Space Saving Technology

To maximize living space in your home, the Cascade slides forward while reclining to create more space between the wall and the chair, requiring only 2.5 inches between the two.

6 Auto Programs

The Titan Cascade 3D offers 6 different special auto massage programs which offer diverse massage experiences to the user.
  • Comfortable Massage The most comfortable massage experience.
  • Relax Massage: A gentle and comfortable deep massage, which can relieve body fatigue and relax the body.
  • Neck & Shoulder Massage: The unique neck and shoulder massage program provides an effective massage of the cervical spine and scapula. Through routine use, neck and shoulder pain can be relieved and neck and shoulder fatigue can be improved.
  • Stretch Massage: The legrest and the backrest expand and contract for stretching of the calves and waist, effectively relieving tiredness, joint soreness, and restoring vitality.
  • Back & Waist Massage: A targeted massage focused on relieving back and waist soreness.
  • Full Body Massage: A deep and comprehensive massage, which can revive body vitality.

Find your favorite massage style by manually selecting between Kneading, Knocking, Tapping, Shiatsu, Sync, or 4D Shiatsu. Additionally, you may manually control your massage’s region, width, speed, and roller intensity.

11 Massage Styles

11 Massage Techniques are available including kneading (7 modes of Kneading can be cyclically switched), Tapping (Tapping I, Tapping II), Simultaneous Kneading and Tapping, Shiatsu (Shiatsu I, Shiatsu II), Grab, 3D (3D I, 3D II, 3D III, 3D IV), etc.

Back and Calf Infrared Heat

The Cascade has heating pads located on the back and calf areas. It offers heat therapy, a great compliment to the roller and airbag massage. Heating the body's temperature is known to increase blood circulation and loosen tense muscles.

Dual Bluetooth Speakers

The Titan Cascade 3D is equipped with Bluetooth technology that reduces the surrounding noise, enhancing your listening experience.

Armrest Shortcut Panel

The side shortcut controller is designed so that the user can easily control the chair without using a remote controller. The user can conveniently adjust the calf frame, reclining position, voice control, auto massage program, and power.

USB Charger and Remote Holder

The Cascade’s armrest is equipped with a remote holder for easy access and convenient placement. Additionally, a USB port can be used to charge your mobile device while you relax.

Slot for Mobile Phone

Never lose your phone again with Cascade’s stylish phone pocket.

Foot Roller Massage

The foot massage rollers are complemented by the comprehensive airbag massage. As the airbags inflate the foot arches are pushed down on the spinning rollers increasing the intensity.

Extendable Footrest

The footrest can extend out up to 7" to provide comfort to a variety of different heights. The footrest is on a spring loaded system where the user can simply push out with the legs to adjust to their desired length.

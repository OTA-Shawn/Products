The Titan Pro Ace II massage chair features an L-Track system with 3D rollers, zero gravity, 6 auto programs, and easy to use remote for the most relaxing massage experience. Packed with unique elbow pads, this chair is ready to give the best user experience. It also offers a unique remote pocket.

3D MASSAGE

The next generation of 3D massage rollers are designed for outstanding range and flexibility. The 3D technology allows the user to control the length of rollers that extend out from the backrest. With 3D technology, reaching and massaging the acupuncture points becomes effortless with accurate precision and depth.

L-Track Massage

As the roller system massages the neck area, it continues through to the lower back and then proceeds to massage the glutes and upper hamstrings. The L-Track mechanism offers about 50% more massage area than the average S-Track massage chairs.

Full Body Air Massage

The Pro Ace II offers a total of 12 airbags in the massage chair. Airbags are located at the shoulders, arms, lower back, feet, and the calf areas. This compression massage helps to continue increasing blood circulation throughout the body to relieve pain and tension.

Unique Foot Rollers

There are spinning reflexology rollers located at the bottom of the footrest providing a soothing kneading massage. When the rollers spin, they will stimulate the acupuncture points along the arch of the feet and as the air bags inflate it will generate a much deeper massage pushing the feet down on the rollers.

Three Steps of Zero Gravity Modes

The Zero gravity position is the perfect position to enjoy a massage, aligning your back relative to your thigh at an optimal level of comfort. The Titan Pro Ace II features a 3 step zero-gravity system, which means that you can customize 3 different angles, whichever the user wants to settle for the best position.

Extendable Footrest

Titan Pro Ace II has an extendable footrest which can be extended up to 7.1 Inches to reach the desired comfortable position which fits a user's various height. Extendable Footrest looks a simple feature, but this helps people to customize their own sized or lengths.

6 Auto Massage Programs

The Titan Pro Ace II has 6 unique per-programmed massage functions. Each offers a different combination of massage styles and focused area. During the each of programs, user can control the level of intensity that user prefers and the suitable level for the user.

4 Massage Styles

The Titan Pro Ace II massage chair comes with manual mode so you can pinpoint a desired massage for a specific area on your body. Pro Ace II can perform Kneading, Tapping, Tapping and Kneading and Shiatsu. You can either pinpoint a specific spot on your back for the massage or massage by sections of the body.

Multi Language Settings

Titan Pro Ace II has 4 multi language choices that you can set it in your own language. You can meet four kinds of language options including English, Traditional Chinese, Simplified Chinese and Korean. The language system is simple and easily accessible to set up your language on the remote control.

Built in Bluetooth Speakers

The Titan Pro Ace II is equipped with Bluetooth technology that allows the user to listen to their favorite music and phone calls on high-quality speakers in the headrest. Music helps the user to calm the body and mind which connects to a relaxing effects on the mind.

Easy to use Remote

The Titan Pro Ace II remote has an easy view screen displaying the function the chair has activated. On the remote it allows you to select the massage type, intensity, speed, and location. You can also operate the functions independently in the Manual Mode.

Heat on Lumbar

The Titan Pro Ace II has 2 heating pads located on the back area, which enhances the effect of the massage. It offers user's body to be warmed and it makes more relaxed and calmer during the massage. Those 2 heating pads help the user can sleep better after auto massage programs.

Elbow and Neck Cushions

Titan Pro Ace II features extra comforts and luxurious quality of cushions to cover the elbow and neck to protect the user from extra pressure. Added extra cushions on the Titan Pro Ace II helps the user to stray more comfortable and safer on the massage chair.

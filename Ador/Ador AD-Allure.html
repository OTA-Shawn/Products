    Ador Allure is a 3D adjustable massage chair with an advanced SL-track rolling system. The Allure massage chair comes furnished with an intelligent voice control, computer body scan, full body airbag massage, 3 steps of Zero Gravity with 21 auto massage programs.

    The Allure includes advanced technology which is an intelligent voice recognition. Voice or speech recognition is the ability of devices to record spoken words or commands by the user. The Ador Allure has a tiny microphone around the Armrest Shortcut area which recognizes 15 different phrases that the user can manage the massage modes by speaking.

    The Allure will perform a body scan to map the key areas of the neck and back to ensure the optimal amount of pressure applied during the massage. The scanning technology will detect your shoulders height, which can be re-calibrated once the scan is complete.

    The 3D Allure comes equipped with 5 levels of 3D Massage Roller Adjustment and Massage Range & Width. The 3D Technology gives you control over the overall intensity of the massage, by moving the rollers in and out from the back of the chair. Unlike most 3D Massage chairs on the market, the chair lets you adjust the 3D Roller depth at any point in the massage, whether you're in an auto mode, or a manual mode. On top of the depth adjustment, there are 5 levels of speed adjustment, and 5 levels of width adjustment available, and 5 levels of Back Massage Intensity.

    The Allure also comes standard with Osaki's latest massage rolling track, which massages from your neck, all the way down to your glutes and upper hamstrings. This is called an "SL" rolling track, and it works with the computer body scan at the beginning of every session to give every user a massage that fits their body.

    The Allure is designed with efficiency in mind, and that includes the entire airbag system in the chair. The Allure offers a total of 32 airbags in the massage chair. The stream-lined design uses less airbags, while still giving a full-body air compression massage. This also increases the longevity and reliability of the chair.

    The Ador 3D Allure is equipped with 3 steps of Zero Gravity, giving the user a weightless feeling during the massage. This gives the user an option on how far back they want their chair to recline automatically, or you can adjust the chair manually with the remote. Different steps of Zero Gravity offers various types including Initial Step, Comfort Step and Deep Step.

    The Allure slides forwards to create more space saving from the wall to allow the chair to be within 2 inches away from the wall. Maximizing living space in your home.

    The Allure offers 21 different special auto massage programs which offers diverse massage experiences to the user; some of these programs include:
    Stretching: Stretch your legs and waist through the leg rest and the backrest.
    Fast: Experience all the massage effects quickly.
    Stress Relieving: Restore the full body from fatigue and pain to achieve relaxation.
    Good-night Sleep: Offering the twelve major acu-points massage for healthcare.
    Spine Care: Care for the health of spine and perform improvements for the lack of exercise.
    Neck & Shoulder Care: Effective and unique massage for the cervical spine and scapula.
    Office Regimen: Helps to increase physical exercise by massage and improve the body functions.

    The 21 auto programs utilize the 6 different massage techniques available in the chair. These 6 massage styles are Kneading, Tapping, Kneading & Tapping, Shiatsu, Shoulder Lifting and 3D.

    The Allure has heating pads located on the Lumbar area. It offers the heat therapy which is a great compliment to the roller and airbag massage. Heating the body's temperature is known to increase blood circulation and loosen tense muscles.

    Located in the back portion of the calf massager are spinning rollers with 1 roller that provides a smooth kneading style massage. On the bottom of the foot massage rollers that spin along the arch of the feet which is complimented by airbag massage. As the airbags inflate the feet are pushed down on the spinning rollers increasing the intensity.

    The Allure is equipped with Bluetooth technology that reduces the surrounding noise, enhancing your listening experience.
    Side panel controller is designed that the user can easily control the chair without using a remote tablet.
    Adore Allure thoughtfully designed with a wireless charger for the modern times of digital devices. The user can charge their smartphone on the wireless charging pad located at the top of the armrest.

    The footrest can be extended up to 6.5" to ensure the optimal leg length for your height. The foot component will extend outwards and then retract inwards. Simply apply pressure with your feet to be at the desired position.

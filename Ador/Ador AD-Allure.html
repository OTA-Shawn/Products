ADOR MASSAGE CHAIR
Ador Allure is a 3D adjustable massage chair with an advanced SL-track rolling system. The Allure massage chair comes furnished with an intelligent voice control, computer body scan, full body airbag massage, 3 steps of Zero Gravity with 21 auto massage programs.

Intelligent
Voice Control
The Ador Allure boasts intelligent voice recognition, enabling users to manage massage modes through 15 different spoken phrases using a tiny microphone near the Armrest Shortcut area.
Computer Body
Scan System
The Allure conducts a body scan to map key neck and back areas, ensuring optimal massage pressure. The scanning tech detects shoulder height and allows recalibration after completing the scan.
Advanced 3D Massage Mechanism
The 3D Allure offers 5 levels of 3D Massage Roller Adjustment and Massage Range & Width. You can control the massage intensity by adjusting roller depth during auto or manual modes, with 5 levels each for speed, width, and back massage intensity.
Ergonomic SL-Track Massage
The Allure features Osaki's latest "SL" rolling track, massaging from the neck to the glutes and upper hamstrings. It works with a computer body scan to customize the massage for each user.
Space Saving Technology
The Allure slides forwards to create more space saving from the wall to allow the chair to be within 2 inches away from the wall. Maximizing living space in your home.
3 Steps Zero Gravity
The Ador 3D Allure has 3 Zero Gravity modes for a weightless massage experience. Users can choose the recline level automatically or adjust it manually with the remote, including Initial, Comfort, and Deep Steps.

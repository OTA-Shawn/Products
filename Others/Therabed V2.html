Introducing the Therabed V2, a next-generation massage bed featuring an advanced 3D massage mechanism. Its 3D rollers move in all directions to deliver a precise, full-body massage that contours to your body and melts away tension. With adjustable intensity, automatic programs, and air compression massage, the Therabed V2 combines comfort, durability, and spa-level relaxation in one sleek design.
3D MASSAGE
MECHANISM
The TheraBed V2’s 3D massage mechanism delivers a deep massage by moving rollers in three directions—up and down, side to side, and in and out—mimicking the precision of human hands to relieve tension and soothe sore muscles.
INTELLIGENT
VOICE COMMAND
The TheraBed V2 includes built-in voice control for a fully hands-free experience. Simply say “Hello Bed” along with a preset command, and enjoy a seamless, uninterrupted massage without lifting a finger.
HEIGHT
ADJUSTMENTS
Using the remote control, the user can adjust the massage up to 7 different heights, making it a customizable massage for every user.
COMPACT DESIGN
The TheraBed V2 boasts a compact, space-saving design—you simply unfold the foot extension when you're ready to stretch out.
CALF & FEET
AIR COMPRESSION
The TheraBed V2 includes a detachable air leg massager that applies rhythmic air compression to improve circulation and relieve lower-body tension.
HEATING THERAPY
The TheraBed V2 features soothing heating therapy in the stomach pad, back area, foot extension area, massage rollers, and the calf area to help relax tight muscles and enhance overall comfort.
*The heating pads provide warmth in the range of approximately 75 - 83°F

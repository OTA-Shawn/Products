Titan D7908 is a sleek and ergonomic office chair designed for comfort and productivity, featuring strong lumbar support and breathable mesh upholstery for extended use.
High Density Foam with Fabric Cover
Titan D7908 holds a seat made out of high density foam, which provides cushion and support for prolonged sitting periods. Specifically, this high quality material is made out of CA117 Fire-proof American Standard Foam, meaning that the foam is treated with fire-retardant chemicals to reduce its flammability.
PP Back Support Frame
Made out of fixed PP, the back support frame provides essential ergonomic support for the user's spine and lower back for prolonged sitting periods. The Back Strength Test of the Titan D7908 results with the chair being able to hold more than 300 lbs.
Fixed PP Armrest
The armrest of D9708 consists of fixed PP, which means it can withstand daily use and maintain their structural integrity over time.
Mesh Back Material
The back of the office chair is made out of mesh, which gives breathability and flexibility to keep users cool and comfortable. Its lightweight nature makes the chair easy to move around while maintaining strength and resilience.
2.0 mm Thickness Black Coating Metal Frame
Titan D7908 utilizes a metal frame to ensure a stable, sound foundation in office environments. The black color adds a sleek and professional aesthetic to the frame, complementing various office decor styles. With a thickness of 2.0 mm, the frame offers sufficient strength to withstand the weight and movement of users while maintaining its structural integrity.
Soft Lumbar Support
Titan D7908 was designed with soft lumbar support to suit different statures while promoting good posture during extended periods of sitting. The contours of the backrest help maintain the natural curve of the spine, reducing strain on the lower back region.

What makes a Double Screen Kiosk so effective?
Maximize your visibility from every direction in high-traffic corridors.
True 360-Degree Visibility
Why choose a direction? With dual back-to-back 55" screens, you can capture the attention of foot traffic walking in both directions simultaneously, doubling your advertising impact without taking up extra floor space.
Effortless USB Plug & Play
No complicated networking required. Simply load your images or videos onto a USB drive, plug it into the kiosk, and the built-in media player automatically loops your content flawlessly.
Mirrored or Independent Playback
Run the exact same promotional video on both sides for uniform branding, or easily configure the kiosk to display different targeted messaging on each side.
Stunning 4K Clarity
Both sides feature an ultra-sharp 4K (3840 x 2160) Grade-A LG Panel. Vivid, bright visuals ensure your messaging pops from across the room, regardless of ambient lighting.
Ultra-Slim, Heavy-Duty Frame
Despite housing two massive 55" displays, the kiosk features a sleek, minimalist profile. Built from commercial-grade materials, it's designed to withstand busy public environments.
Set It & Forget It Reliability
Built for 24/7 continuous looping. Features automatic power on/off scheduling, so your digital signage wakes up with your business and shuts down safely after hours automatically.

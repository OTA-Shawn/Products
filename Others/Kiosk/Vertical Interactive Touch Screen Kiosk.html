Use your finger to tap, scroll, navigate, and engage directly on the screen. Perfectly calibrated for smooth, smartphone-like responsiveness that invites users to interact with your content.

Powered by an Intel i5 processor and Windows 11 Pro. Install and run proprietary software, popular websites, or industry-standard applications natively-no complicated workarounds needed.

Draw attention with an ultra-sharp 4K (2160 x 3840) Grade-A LG Panel. Vivid, crisp visuals ensure your digital signage and interactive directories are bright and readable from any angle.

Equipped with an Intel 8th Generation OPS (Open Pluggable Specification), meaning the computing module can be easily removed, upgraded, or serviced without disassembling the kiosk.
Sleek, Floor-Standing Footprint
Delivers bold, full-height visuals that command attention without congesting your space. The minimalist freestanding design hides cables for a polished, professional aesthetic.
Clear from Every Angle
Featuring a 178° viewing angle and a 1200:1 contrast ratio, ensuring that whether a user is standing right in front of it or glancing from the side, the screen remains clear and colorful.

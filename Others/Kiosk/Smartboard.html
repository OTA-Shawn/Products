Product Utility
What does a Smart Board actually do?
Everything explained in plain language — no tech jargon required.
Write & Draw Interactively
Use your finger or stylus to write, sketch, annotate, and erase directly on screen — like a whiteboard, but everything saves automatically. No markers, no mess.
Mirror Any Device Wirelessly
Cast your laptop, phone, or tablet to the board in seconds. Share a presentation, video, or website with everyone in the room — no cables needed.
Runs the Apps Schools Already Love
Install and run the most popular classroom tools directly on the board — no extra devices needed. Everything your teachers and students already know, now on a giant interactive screen.
Display 4K Content Vividly
Stream videos, display Scripture, show presentations, or run digital signage in stunning 4K clarity — bright and readable from across any room.
Multi-User Touch Collaboration
Up to 20 simultaneous touch points — multiple people interact at once. Perfect for group activities, team brainstorming, and interactive worship.
Built for Zoom, Teams & Video Conferencing
Host seamless hybrid meetings directly from the board. With a built-in array microphone, dual 15W speakers, and native compatibility with Zoom, Microsoft Teams, and Google Meet — your team stays connected without extra equipment.
Anti-Glare Display for Any Lighting Condition
Premium tempered anti-glare glass with a matte finish coating eliminates reflections and reduces eye strain — even in brightly lit classrooms or rooms with direct sunlight. A 178° wide viewing angle and 400-nit brightness ensure everyone in the room sees a crisp, clear image.
Annotate Over Anything in Real Time
Draw, highlight, and write directly over any app, website, video, or live document on screen — without ever leaving what you're viewing. Circle key data in a spreadsheet, underline important lines, mark up a patient diagram, or highlight code during a training session.

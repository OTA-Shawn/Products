HIGH-QUALITY LEATHAIRE MATERIAL
Serene uses a high-quality material called Leathaire. The material offers the luxurious look and feel of genuine leather while being durable and easy to maintain. Leathaire consists of a blend of different materials that are treated to mimic the texture and appearance of real leather. This material is known for its softness, breathability, and resistance to stains, making it an excellent choice for both comfort and style.

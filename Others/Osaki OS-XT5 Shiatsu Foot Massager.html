You’ve asked, and we’ve answered! Introducing the all new Osaki XT5 Kneading Foot Massager. Now you can get all of the benefits of a traditional massage chair’s foot massage, without any of the bulk or commitment. Simply place your XT5 in front of your sofa, office chair, or patio chair and experience true relaxation.
Massage Modes
When using your new XT5, you’ll find the ability to easily cycle through three distinct massage modes including: Comfort, Ease, and Improve. Each of this unique modes target parts of the foot with a variety of massage techniques.
Kneading Rollers
After nearly half a decade of research, the main function of the XT5 does not disappoint. Its advanced kneading rollers work on multiple axises to ensure the optimal massage across your toes, soles, and ankles. These rollers intelligently target acupressure points across a broad spectrum to relieve tension and improve blood flow.
22 Cell Compression Therapy
Located throughout the XT5 are a number of compression cells; compression along the calves imitates the actions of a real masseuse, while the cells along the feet improve contact with the lower rollers.
Calming Heat Therapy
Improve blood flow and circulation through the use of the XT5’s radiating heat therapy. This calming warmth relieves muscle tension to leave you feeling rejuvenated after your massage.
Automatic Massage Timer
Confidently fall asleep during your massage with the XT5’s intelligent timer. This safety feature ensures your device automatically powers off after 15 minutes of continuous use, saving your device from overuse, your feet from being overworked, and your wallet from the electricity bill. You have the option to increase the timer by 5 minutes, for up to 30 minutes of continuous use!
Intuitive Control Panel
Quickly and efficiently control all of your XT5’s functions through the use of the conveniently placed control panel. Access important features like toggling power, cycling massage modes, activating heat therapy, extending the massage timer, changing the pressure intensity, and using the kneading rollers.
Washable Fabric Inserts
Never worry about stains again! The XT5 comes with easily removable fabric covers, meaning you can quickly detach and wash them without damaging the device itself.

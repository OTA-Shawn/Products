3D Skin Analyzer System

An advanced diagnostic imaging device that captures high-resolution, multi-layer skin data to assess conditions such as wrinkles, pigmentation, pores, texture, and hydration.

"Visual Progress Tracking: Create personalized treatment plans and track patient progress over time with precision data."

This system enables practitioners to provide evidence-based consultations, visualizing skin concerns that are not visible to the naked eye, and tailoring skincare regimens for optimal results.

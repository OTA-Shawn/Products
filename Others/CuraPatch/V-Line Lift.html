How to Use
STEPS
  • 1.
    Prepare: Remove the film from the mask.
  • 2.
    Apply: Place the mask on a clean face, stretch the center to fit the chin, and hang it gently on your ears.
  • 3.
    Wait: Leave for 20–30 minutes (up to 1 hour).
  • 4.
    Remove & Dispose: Remove the mask and discard it after use (one-time use only).

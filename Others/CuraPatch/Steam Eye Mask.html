How to Use
STEPS
  • 1.
    Remove eye mask from the pouch. Use immediately upon opening as the mask begins to warm up instantly.
  • 2.
    Gently tear along perforation, then place elastic bands over ears and white area of the mask over eyes.
  • 3.
    Relax for 30 minutes.
  • 4.
    Remove mask and dispose it in the trash. Do not resuse. Do not reuse
USAGE TIPS

Keep eyes closed while wearing the eye mask

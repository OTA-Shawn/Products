3D Massage

The next generation of 3D massage rollers are designed for outstanding range and flexibility. The 3D technology allows the user to control the length of rollers that extend out from the backrest. OS-Monarch has 4 levels of 3D intensity control.

L-Track Massage & Body Scan System

As the roller system massages the neck area, it continues through to the lower back and then proceeds to massage the glutes and upper hamstrings.
The Monarch will perform a body scan to map the key areas of the neck and back to ensure the optimal amount of pressure applied during the massage. The scanning technology will detect your shoulders height, which can be re-calibrated once the scan is complete.

Space Saving Technology

Osaki's innovative design team has engineered a space saving technology that slides the chair forward as the chair reclines. As a result, OS-Monarch takes only one inch to get to the fully reclined position.

Built in Bluetooth Speakers

The Osaki OS-Monarch is equipped with Bluetooth technology that allows the user to listen to their favorite music and phone calls on high quality speakers in the headrest.

Spring Loaded Extendable Footrest

Unique Foot Roller Massage

USB Connector

You can enjoy your massage with your smart phone, i-Pad, or any other electronic products charging with built in USB connector at the armrest of chair.

4 Massage Styles

The Osaki OS-Monarch massage chair comes with manual mode so you can pinpoint a desired massage for a specific area on your body. OS-Monarch can perform Kneading, Knocking, Tapping, and Shiatsu. Auto Massage Program

The OS-Monarch has 5 different auto massage programs plus 4 more special massage programs. There is Recovery, Pain relief, Stretch, Sleeping and Experience program. For special, there is Athlete mode, Shopaholic, Neck & Shoulder and Back & Waist.

Manual Massage Setting

You can meet variety of options to custom your massage. OS-Monarch has Manual control function for Mode, Position, Width, Speed and 3D. Each category has many options to adjust. You can also control Air, Foot Roller, Timer setting and Heating.

Multi Language Support

OS-Monarch offers multi language choices to users. You can select your language at "Setting" - "Language". There is Chinese, English and Korean as an option.

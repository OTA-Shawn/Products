Featuring a 3D massage mechanism, L-track design, intelligent body scanning, lumbar heat therapy, zero gravity reclining, full body compression massage, specialized foot massage, intuitive remote control, and multi-lingual support.
Body Scan System
The Monarch will perform a body scan to map the key areas of the neck and back to ensure the optimal amount of pressure applied during the massage. The scanning technology will detect your shoulders height, which can be re-calibrated once the scan is complete.
3D Massage
The next generation of 3D massage rollers are designed for outstanding range and flexibility. The 3D technology allows the user to control the length of rollers that extend out from the backrest. OS-Monarch has 4 levels of 3D intensity control.
L-Track Massage
As the roller system massages the neck area, it continues through to the lower back and then proceeds to massage the glutes and upper hamstrings.
Space Saving Technology
Osaki's innovative design team has engineered a space saving technology that slides the chair forward as the chair reclines. As a result, OS-Monarch takes only one inch to get to the fully reclined position.
Intelligent Body Scanning
Inspired by NASA technology, the Zero gravity position is the perfect position to enjoy a massage, aligning your back relative to your thigh at an optimal level of comfort. When reclined into the zero-gravity position, the complete weight of your back is virtually supported by the backrest which maximizes the intensity of the massage.
Air Massage
There are multiple airbags located throughout the massage chair to offer a full body massage experience. Airbags are located at the shoulders, the arms, the feet, and the calf areas. This compression massage helps to continue increasing blood circulation throughout the body to relieve pain and tension.
Lumbar Heat Therapy
With Monarch’s heat shawl lumbar therapy, the incorporated graphene effectively warm and help ease soreness in the lower back area.

As the rollers spin it will stimulate acupuncture points and as the air bags inflate it will generate a much deeper massage along the bottoms of your feet.

The Osaki OS-Monarch is equipped with Bluetooth technology that allows the user to listen to their favorite music and phone calls on high quality speakers in the headrest.

The Osaki OS-Monarch remote is an easy view screen displaying the functions the chair has activated. On the remote it allows you to select the massage type, intensity, speed and location. You can also operate the functions independently in manual mode.

The 4000LE 3D Massage Chair blends intelligent innovation with deep, immersive comfort to deliver a truly elevated relaxation experience. Equipped with the AI Series Intelligent Health Detection system, it analyzes your body’s tension and automatically customizes each session for precise, personalized relief. Its advanced 3D + Sonic Massage technology combines adjustable depth with rhythmic vibration to penetrate muscles more effectively, easing stress and promoting full-body rejuvenation. Paired with a 2-stage Zero Gravity reclining system, the 4000LE gently positions your body for optimal weight distribution, reducing spinal pressure and enhancing circulation—so you can fully unwind in a state of weightless comfort.

The Osaki 4000LE gathers biometric data through your palm while the rollers glide along your spine, measuring heart rate, HRV, muscle tension, posture, and microcirculation. Using this information, the AI system customizes the massage in real time to maximize effectiveness and deliver a more personalized experience.
3D+ Sonic Massage
The all new Sonic Pulse massage is highly effective and pairs seamlessly with traditional massage techniques. It helps relax muscles more quickly, allowing the other massage functions to work deeper and more efficiently. This may support faster muscle recovery while promoting better circulation and overall relaxation.

Measuring Guide
  • Calculate the doorway width by subtracting 2 inches from the smallest inner width of the door frame. (1 / 2 inch from each side)
  • Please use a standard measuring tape to measure the doorway.
  • If the door cannot be fully opened, open it as much as possible and measure the width from the edge of the door hinge.
  • If there is a rounded curve on the wall, calculate the inner width based on the most protruding point of the wall.

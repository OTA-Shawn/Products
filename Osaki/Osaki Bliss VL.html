Shoulder Position Adjustment
The Bliss allows the rollers to move not only up and down, but also left and right. This technology enables the chair to simulate the experience of human hands while allowing the user to adjust the speed in three levels and the width in three increments.
Convenient Remote Controller
The Bliss includes a remote controller for easy navigation and adjustment settings.
SL-track System
The SL-Track is made up of Laser-Crafted Steel. The track allows the massage rollers to effortlessly reach all the way from the head to the hip areas.
Air Compression Hip Massage
The Bliss incorporates 2 air cells in the hip area to provide a relaxing air compression massage. This massage can be adjusted in two air intensity levels .
Easy Reclining Position
The Bliss massage chair allows you to recline back to about a 60 degree angle . By turning the knob under the chair, you are able to adjust the tension to bring the chair back up to the upright position.
Lumbar Heat Therapy
The carbon coil in the Bliss will effectively warm and help release tension in your lower back area.

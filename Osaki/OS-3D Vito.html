INTELLIGENT HEALTH DETECTION
The Osaki Vito is incorporated with intelligent health detection, which continuously monitors the user's muscle fatigue. Based on real-time data, massage techniques are adjusted to suit the user's current physical conditions and to help improve overall health. This technology aims to offer a more personalized and health-conscious massage experience.
INTELLIGENT
VOICE CONTROL
The Osaki Vito massage chair can recognize a wide range of voice commands , allowing users to control and adjust the massage chair hands-free, enhancing convenience and user experience.
3D MASSAGE MECHANISM
The Osaki Vito features 3D Massage Mechanism, which enables the rollers to move in and out, in addition to up and down, and left and right . This advanced technology system mimics the hands of a professional masseuse, providing a deeper and more precise massage. It can adjust to the contours of the body and offers customizable intensity for a personalized massage experience.
AUTOMATIC BODY SCAN
The Osaki Vito massage chair uses advanced body scanning technology to detect and map the user's body shape and size , ensuring a customized and effective massage experience for every user.

The Osaki Vito is designed with a touch pad controller, which offers an intuitive and user-friendly interface customizing the massage experience. With a simple touch, users can easily adjust massage settings with seamless operation.

LUMBAR and CALF HEAT THERAPY
The Osaki Vito allows the user to apply soothing warmth to the lower back and calf areas, helping to relax tense muscles and improve blood circulation.
SL-TRACK SYSTEM
The Osaki Vito's SL-track system enables massage rollers to seamlessly cover your entire body. The SL-Track System ensures comprehensive massage coverage by extending from your neck and shoulders to your hamstrings .
ZERO GRAVITY and SPACE SAVING
Originally pioneered by NASA, the Zero Gravity technology in the Osaki Vito is designed for the user to recline into a state of weightlessness to relieve pressure on the body. In a complete Zero Gravity position, the chair can be placed 6 inches away from the way and still transition into a full recline.
SPRING LOADED 7” EXTENDABLE FOOTREST
The Osaki Vito’s Spring-loaded Extendable Footrest offers adjustable length up to 7 inches , accomodating various leg lengths and ensuring optimal comfort during use.

FOOT ROLLER

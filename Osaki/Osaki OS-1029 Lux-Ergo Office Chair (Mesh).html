  • The Osaki 1029 Lux-Ergo Office Chair brings a whole new level of customizable comfort to your home or office. Control the backrest angle, backrest height, armrest position, degree of seat tilt, and seat height.     One-Touch Height & Tilt System

    While sitting, reach down alongside the seat to easily make alterations to your experience. Manually make micro adjustments to the angle of your backrest and seat, as well as quickly fine tune the height of the entire chair.

    Backrest Tilt System

    This synchro tilt mechanism smoothly reclines the user and allows you to lock-in virtually any position.

    Seat Tilt System

    Osaki’s unique seat tilt mechanism relieves hip and backrest pain by allowing the user to choose their most comfortable posture.

    Seat Height Adjustment

    Raise or lower your chair to comfortably sit at any desk. This easy-use system allows for more than half a foot of adjustment up or down.

    Adjustable Armrest Height

    Whether you’re looking for the perfect support, or simply want to easily slide the chair under your desk, the Lux-Ergo Office Chair’s adjustable contoured armrests quickly conform to your needs.

    Adjustable Backrest Height

    The cushioned backrest encompasses your back, perfectly aligning your spine. This tempered system helps to reduce fatigue after long hours at your desk.

    High Quality Mesh Seat & Backrest

    The seat cushion consists of high quality upholstery and PU injection mold foam, embracing your body as to ensure the utmost pleasant experience even over late nights at the office.

    360 Degrees Swivel

    The updated five-star chair base is stronger than other comparable models and has a higher weight capacity of roughly 270 pounds.

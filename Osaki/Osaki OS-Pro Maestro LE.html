Body Scan Technology
The OS-4D Pro Maestro LE will perform a body scan to map the key areas of the neck and back to ensure the optimal amount of pressure applied during the massage. The scanning technology will detect your shoulders height, which can be re-calibrated once the scan is complete.
Touchscreen Controller
The remote controller is upgraded to a larger screen for user’s convenience to operate the massage chair. It has an easy view screen displaying the function the chair has activated. It also has magnetic holder that fixes the remote into place.
4D Massage Mechanism
The 4D massage allows another experience with account for the speed and rhythm of the massage. The Osaki OS-4D Pro Maestro LE provides 8 levels of adjustment for the 4D massage with heated back rollers.
SL-track Technology
The SL-Track is made up of Laser-Crafted Steel. The track allows the massage rollers to cover the entirety of your body. The SL-Track reaches all the way from your neck and shoulders to your hamstrings.

The DuoMax is a 4D massage chair designed to provide an unparalleled relaxation experience. The DuoMax incorporates intelligent sensors that scan various metrics, tailoring the massage to individual preferences and targeting specific areas.
Intelligent Health Detection
The DuoMax has advanced capabilities with its built-in health monitor. By detecting various metrics such as heart rate, blood oxygen levels, and fatigue index, the chair's Ai technology can provide a personalized massage experience tailored to your body's current condition.
Dual 4D Massage Mechanism
With its 4D massage mechanism, users can enjoy a wide range of massage techniques that simulate human-like movements. Its quad precision rollers can adjust their depth and speed, ensuring precise and effective relief for muscle tension and stress.
2 Heating Cores
Integrating heated jade cores that move along the contours of your body, this function delivers targeted relief by combining the benefits of massage and heat.
SL Flex Track
The DuoMax breaks the boundaries of a standard SL-track by incorporating a level of flexibility. The SL Flex Track holds the ability to be at a fixed angle relative to the backrest. This new innovation of a flexible track system is implemented while providing full coverage of the body from your neck and shoulders to your hamstrings.
Smart Voice Control
With Avalon's voice control feature, you can easily utilize the chair without having to lift a finger. Just follow the guidelines and commands in the manual and the chair will respond with fast reactivity.

36 Air Cells Massage
The DuoMax includes a total of 36 airbags in the massage chair, providing coverage to shoulders, arms, hips, calves, and feet.

and Space Saving Technology

Body Heat Therapy
The DuoMax’s heat therapy can help warm and help ease soreness in the lower back area. The DuoMax also targets heat along the chest and stomach area through the heating shawl.

Osaki 3D Dreamer V2
How do you make the perfect massage chair better? That’s the problem Osaki’s development team were faced with when they began redesigning our best-selling massage chair. To tackle this problem we added a slew of luxury features, such as heated massage rollers, an automatic legrest, and full body compression therapy to further compound the Dreamer’s perfection.

3D Massage Mechanism
Experience a new dimension of luxury with The Dreamer’s three-dimensional massage rollers. When used in tandem with the chair’s SL-Track your body will finally know true relaxation.
Through the use of a scientifically-engineered SL-Track, the massage rollers can extend from the base of the skull to the very edge of your hamstrings.

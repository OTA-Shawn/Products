The Osaki OS-7200H Pinnacle has a sleek design, a comfortable feel, and a roller with a wide range of settings and capabilities.  Equipped with quad style rollers, the OS-7200H Pinnacle can penetrate deeply into your muscle tissuebody scan that will customize the massage for each user. With the Osaki OS-7200H Pinnacle, you can sit down, choose a setting, get comfortable, and relax.

Intelligent Body Scanning

The Computer Body Scan allows the rollers to scan the curvature of one's back. Regardless of your body type, this highly advanced chair will self-adjust making each massage experience feel customized.

Automatic Extendable Footrest

The calf and foot massager is capable of making adjustments according to each individual's leg length, ensuring that the massage air bags are concentrating on the correct areas.

2-Stage Zero Gravity

This Zero Gravity position will be able to provide a much deeper tissue massage. When reclined in this position, the pressure is relieved off the back enhancing the massage experience. This perfect position aligns your back relative to your thigh at an optimal level of comfort. When reclined into the zero gravity position, the complete weight of your back is virtually supported by the backrest. This feature improves spinal decompression and decreases muscle tension of the spine. There are 2 stages of zero gravity. The therapeutic effects are incredibly effective and may be felt immediately.

Chromotherapy

LED blue ambient lights on the side panels create a chromo-therapeutic environment when in a dark room.

Quad Roller Head Massage

The OS-7200H PINNACLE is equipped with twin rollers to mimic the feel of human thumbs & fingers, enhancing the massage experience.

51 Air Bags Full Body Compression

Fifty-one air bags are strategically placed to massage the vital areas such as the neck, shoulder, back, arms, hands, calves, feet, and the lumbar area.

Specialized Foot Massage

Located on the bottom of the feet are 2 rows of spinning reflexology massager which provide a soothing kneading style application. As the rollers spin it will stimulate acupuncture points and as the air bags inflate it will generate a much deeper massage along the bottoms of your feet.

Wireless Remote Control

Located on the top right of the main remote, you will find a compact wireless remote allowing you to control the main features of the chair without having to struggle to reach the main remote in the reclined position.

Heat Therapy

Located along the shoulder are 2 heating pads to apply heat, to loosen muscle tissue so that the rollers can apply a more effective and deeper massage. This may help relieve mild stress and tension in the neck and shoulders.

Full Size Remote Control

The Osaki OS-7200H Pinnacle remote presents one of the largest viewing screens making it easy view what functions the chair has activated. There is also a flip down cover to access the manual functions of the chair. The remote allows you to select the massage type, intensity, speed and location. You can also independently select the area of airbag massage. The manual mode massage includes kneading, tapping, shiatsu, rolling, combination and Swedish.

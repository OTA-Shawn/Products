Vanguard Duo is engineered to deliver a next-generation wellness experience through Real Dynamic Brushless 4D + 4D dual massage mechanisms, intelligent A.I. body pain detection, and full-body air compression technology. Designed with a SL Flex Track system, Zero Gravity positioning, lumbar & knee heating, and immersive Bluetooth audio, it provides deeply personalized relaxation and full-body recovery. Complete with a 12.3” touchscreen controller, voice control, and wireless charging, Vanguard Duo blends advanced technology with everyday comfort.
Health detection
The Vanguard Duo massage chair features an advanced health monitoring system that detects key body metrics including fatigue index, heart rate, blood oxygen levels, microcirculation, and heart rate variability. Using this data, its AI-driven technology creates a personalized massage experience tailored to your body's current condition.
3-Step Instructions
step1
Turn on & Connect
Pick up the sensor, press the power button once, and wait for the top light to turn blue to confirm the connection
step2
Start Detection
Follow the on-screen instructions. Place your middle finger on the red light and tap “Start Detection”
step3
View Results & Start Massage
Once detection is complete, review your health data and begin a personalized massage tailored to your body’s condition.
Intelligent off-line Voice control
The Vanguard Duo' features an intelligent voice control that allows users to control the massage experience hands-free. Simply use voice commands to adjust massage settings in a seamless manner.
Automatic Body Scan System
The Vanguard Duo’s advanced automatic body scan technology detects your body’s unique contours, shoulder position, and massage pressure level to deliver a more customized, comfortable, and effective massage experience.
4D + 4D
Massage mechanism
The advanced 4D+4D dual massage mechanism offers a range of techniques that mimic human-like movements. The quad precision rollers adjust in depth and speed, providing targeted relief for muscle tension, with separate controls for each mechanism.
For added comfort, the rollers also feature heating, blending therapeutic warmth with precise massage for a truly soothing experience. Heated rollers glide along your natural contours, combining therapeutic warmth and precision massage for targeted relief and unmatched comfort.
Dynamic brushless 4D motor
Experience next-generation precision with Osaki’s Dynamic Brushless 4D roller system. Built on advanced four-dimensional motion, it features horizontal flat-axis expansion, increasing the massage width to over 9.1 inches for a broader, more immersive experience. The upgraded brushless motor delivers whisper-quiet performance, smoother transitions, and long-term reliability.

SL - flex Track System
The Vanguard Duo revolutionizes the standard SL-track with its innovative SL Flex Track system, offering enhanced flexibility for superior adaptability and comfort. This advanced design maintains a fixed angle relative to the backrest for seamless support, while the flexible SL-track provides an intensive stretch and extended coverage from the neck and shoulders down to the hamstrings.
Zero Gravity
and 2 modes
Inspired by NASA-developed technology, the Vanguard Duo features Zero Gravity with two reclining modes, Leisure and Cinema, allowing users to experience a true weightless sensation for ultimate relaxation. Recline positions are fully customizable, with the option to use automatic presets or fine-tune manually through the intuitive remote control.

Measuring Guide
  • Calculate the doorway width by subtracting 2 inches from the smallest inner width of the door frame. (1 / 2 inch from each side)

  • If the door cannot be fully opened, open it as much as possible and measure the width from the edge of the door hinge.
  • If there is a rounded curve on the wall, calculate the inner width based on the most protruding point of the wall.

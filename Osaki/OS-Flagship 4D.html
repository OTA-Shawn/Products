4D MASSAGE MECHANISM
The Flagship offers advanced 4D massage system, transcending traditional massages by allowing the user to not only adjust the intensity, speed, and width of massage rollers but also the depth controlling the intensity . The variating speed of the roller heads create a more lifelike experience.
TECHNOLOGY
By utilizing the build-in health monitor, the Flagship gathers data for the Ai driven system to detect tension and stress in your torso area. Based on this data, the Flagship can create a customized massage session to your specific needs and preferences.
INTELLIGENT HEALTH MANAGEMENT
The Flagship massage chair features a build-in health monitor, which detects various metrics such as fatigue index, heart rate, blood oxygen, and microcirculation. With this data, the chair’s Ai technology can provide a personalized massage experience tailored to your body’s current condition .
TOUCHSCREEN CONTROL
The Flagship includes a remote controller, allowing the user to easily navigate and adjust massage settings. The flagship includes a wireless rechargeable remote tablet , allowing the user to freely operate the massage chair.
AUTOMATIC BODY SCAN
The automatic body scan feature in the Flagship precisely measures the user's unique shape and size , customizing the massage experience for optimal comfort and effectiveness.
RELAXING AROMATHERAPY
The Flagship includes a series of Aroma cartridges that can be turned on using the controller. Located next to the speakers, the refillable Aroma capsule releases a scent that may help the user ease and relax into the massage experience.

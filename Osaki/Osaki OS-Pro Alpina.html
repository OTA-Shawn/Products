Advanced Massage Mechanism
The advanced message mechanism lets you adjust the speed and width of the rollers at any time in a massage, whether you are in auto or manual mode.
Touchscreen Remote
The Osaki OS-Pro Alpina is integrated with a touchscreen remote that allows you to see all the functions along with the automatic programs. The touchscreen is able to switch between English, Chinese, Spanish, and French.
Auto Body Scan
Before initiating any automatic program, the Osaki OS-Pro Alpina will conduct a body scan and map the key areas of the neck and back to ensure a consistent amount of pressure. The scanning technology will also detect your shoulder height, which can be recalibrated once the scan is complete.
Ache Sensor
Sensors for your fingers rest in the armrest of the Osaki OS-Pro Alpina massage chair. This helps the massage chair to detect points where you have tension to focus your massage where you need it most.
SL-track Technology
The SL-Track is made up of Laser-Crafted Steel. The track allows the massage rollers to cover the entirety of your body. The SL-Track reaches all the way from your neck and shoulders to your hamstrings.
Zero Gravity Reclining
Inspired by NASA, the Zero Gravity position is the perfect position for ultimate relaxation. When you are placed in the Zero Gravity position, the weight of your back is virtually supported by the backrest which reduces compression along your spine. Your weight add to the intensity of the massage.

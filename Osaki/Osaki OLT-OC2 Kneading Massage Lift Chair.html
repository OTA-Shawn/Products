Osaki OLT-OC2 is a unique luxury lift chair that provides kneading massage. The Osaki lift chair is fitted with the highest quality German motor which help the user getting in and out of a chair easily. OLT-OC2 lift chair is equipped 10 stationary kneading points, a first of kind that offers a kneading massage on back and legs. The massage is complimented by a heating pad on back seat cushion that distributes a warm heat that enhance the massage experience. The chair can hold up to 350lbs and moves quietly as it lifts and reclines.
Flat Recline
Unlike any other massage chairs, OLT-OC2 power massaging lift chair provides a Flat Recline Feature. With this feature, users can literally lay flat and get a massage unlike any other lifting chair. The massage can be complimented with a warm heat by simply activating it using the remote control.
Okin Motors
Osaki use only the best parts and the lifting chair is no exception. The OKIN motor is German engineered delivering a quiet action with smooth movements.
Lifting Up & Down
The easy to use remote allows the user to automatically move to help them get seated, reclined and to stand. The reclines to approximately 160 degrees.
Reclining
As the OLT-OC2 Lifting chair reclines, the leg ottoman will raise up providing support to the legs.
Back & Calf Kneading Massage
The kneading leg massagers are located at the center of the ottoman. The rotating action provides a soothing massage to the calf area depending on the height of the user.
Back Heating
Heating pads are located in the seat and mid-lower back. The warm heat application enhances the massage experience and the same time loosen muscles to help relax. The heating feature can be turned on or off.
Remote Controller
OLT-OC2 luxury lift chair has a simple and easy to use remote control. The remote controller has a LED screen with blue back light to make it visible in the dark. As the chair reclines, the stronger the massage will become with more body weight being applying to the massage heads.
Side Pocket
On the side of the massaging lift chair are large pockets on both sides to store away the remote, magazines and any other articles you may want to have at your disposal when using the massage chair.
Easy Remove Back Cushion
The Osaki massaging lift chair comes with an additional pad for users that prefer a milder massage. If the user wishes to make the massage stronger simply remove the pad that is attached by velcro.
Water Resistant Material
The Osaki Lift Chair upholstery is coated with a water resistant spray that prevents from liquids to automatically absorb in the fabric. The liquid will bead off at contact and roll off. If the liquid is padded down the liquid will absorb into the fabric so if liquids are spilled, the user should remove themselves from the chair immediately and proceed to wipe down. The water resistant coating is to add a layer of protection and hyggiene.

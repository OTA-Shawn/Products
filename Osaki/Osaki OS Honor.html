Introducing Osaki’s latest 3D massage chair that has been in the works for many years, carefully engineered and designed in Japan. The chair is thoughtfully designed for comfort and an incredible massage experience. The innovative brushless motors make it one of the most reliable and quiet motors in the industry. Decades of Japanese massage techniques and technology has been poured into the Osaki 3D Honor Massage Chair. The chair has been developed with the only the user in mind. With years of feedback from our valued customers the evolution of massage chairs is displayed in this unique model.

3D Massage Mechanism

The Osaki 3D Honor is equipped with the lasted generation of 3D massage technology. The 3D features allow for the massage rollers to extend out and retract in on the backrest. The massage head 3D function will dictate the intensity of the massage. As the rollers extend out, the stronger the massage will be. On the remote this feature can be easy controlled by pressing the 3D+ or the 3D- button.

Backrest Scanning System

The Osaki 3D Honor computer body scan has an upgraded 3 core processor to improve the accuracy and speed of mapping the back prior to delivering an incredible massage. The starting point of the massage can be adjusted. Right after the computer scan is completed you have a 5 second window to raise or lower the massage starting point. The body scan will map out he curvature of the back to ensue that consistent amount of massage pressure is being applied top to bottom.

SL-Track

The SL-Track has length of 52” starting from the neck to down through the glutes to the hamstrings.

Zero Gravity Mode

Inspired by NASA technology, the Zero gravity position is the perfect position to enjoy a massage, aligning your back relative to your thigh at an optimal level of comfort. When reclined into the zero-gravity position, the complete weight of your back is virtually supported by the backrest which maximizes the intensity of the massage. In this position when the knees are brought above the chest will optimize your blood flow, in order to relax the body more.

Space Saving

Osaki's innovative design team has engineered a space saving technology that slides the chair forward as the chair reclines. As a result, the OS Honor takes only 4.5 inches to get to the fully reclined position.

Full Body Air Massages

There are multiple airbags located throughout the massage chair to offer a full body massage experience. The Honor offers a total of 14 highly efficient airbags in the massage chair. Airbags are located at the shoulders, arms, lower back, feet, and the calf areas. This compression massage promotes blood circulation and muscle relaxation. The latest engineering has dramatically reduced the airbag count making the chair far more efficient and increasing reliability without compromising massage coverage.

Heating on Lumbar

The Osaki Honor has heating pads located on the Lumbar area. It offers the heat therapy which is a great compliment to the roller and airbag massage. Heating the body’s temperature is known to increase blood circulation and loosen tense muscles.

Foot Roller Massage

OS 3D Honor has 2 spinning reflexology massage rollers located on the bottom of the feet that provide a soothing kneading style application. As the rollers spin it will stimulate acupuncture points and as the air bags inflate it will generate a much deeper massage along the bottoms of your feet. It also has ankle grip function that heightens your foot massage experience.

Extendable Legrest

Osaki OS 3D honor has Extendable Footrest which can be extended up to 7 Inches to reach the desired comfortable position which fits a user’s various height.

6 Auto Massage Programs

The Osaki Honor has 6 unique pre-programmed massage functions. Each offering different combination of massage styles and focused area. Neck / Shoulder, Strengthen, Relax, Thai, Recover, Sleeping

Easy Remote Control

The Osaki Honor remote has an easy view screen displaying the function the chair has activated. On the remote it allows you to select the massage type, intensity, speed, and location. You can also operate the functions independently in the Manual Mode. The user can customize massage by pressing Spot or Partial Back Massage button which allows the user to select spot massage for any specific part of the back.

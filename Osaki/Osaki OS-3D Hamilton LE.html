Osaki 3D Hamilton LE
Meet Osaki’s newest and the most customizable massage chair: The Hamilton LE. With this chair you will experience a comprehensive full body massage with a greater reach, and deeper relaxation, and higher quality components. The Hamilton LE comes with adjustable 3D Rollers, 21 Massage Programs, Intelligent Body Scanning, Zero Gravity Reclining, and a Specialized Footrest Massage to boot!

Voice Controller
You can easily interact with your massage through the sound of your voice. Simply say, “Hey Alice,” or press the button on the armrest, to initiate the voice control. The Hamilton LE will recognize 15 important commands.
3D Massage Mechanism
Our adjustable 3D massage rollers allow you to customize your massage between 5 speed levels, 5 intensity levels, and 5 width ranges. Experiment with your settings to craft the perfect massage specifically for you.
Sturdy SL-Track
The Hamilton LE comes with a sturdy SL-track that combines the benefits of traditional massage tracks. With its intelligent body scanning system, the Hamilton maps your shoulders and spine in order to provide an exceptional massage experience.
Zero Gravity
Reclining
Osaki’s 3 steps zero gravity reclining places you in the most desirable sleeping posture; this calming position decreases pressure on your spine and eases muscle tension.
Space Saving
Technology
To utilize minimal space, the Hamilton requires only 2 inches away from the nearest wall. Our space saving technology allows you to maximize floor space, without needing an entire room for a singular massage chair.
32 Cell
Full Body
Air Massage
Equipped with a full-body compression massage, the Hamilton allows control over 5 levels of adjustable intensity. A total 32 air cells are located throughout the chair, from shoulders and arms, to hips, calves, and feet.
Lumbar Heating
Heat therapy is provided to the lower lumbar, which helps to improve blood circulation and relieve back tension. As soon as you activate lumbar heating, you will feel your muscles relaxed and eased.
Intelligent Controls
Some people prefer soft massages while others prefer them much stronger. The Hamilton LE allows you to adjust the massage to your specific needs. With the ability to choose from 21 auto modes and 6 different massage techniques, as well as adjust between 5 speed intensities and 5 degrees of width.

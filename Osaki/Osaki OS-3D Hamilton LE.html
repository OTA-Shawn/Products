Voice Control

You can easily interact with your massage through the sound of your voice. Simply say, “Hey Alice,” or press the button on the armrest, to initiate the voice control. The Hamilton LE will recognize 15 important commands.

3D Massage Mechanism

Our adjustable 3D massage rollers allow you to customize your massage between 5 speed levels, 5 intensity levels, and 5 width ranges. Experiment with your settings to craft the perfect massage specifically for you.

Sturdy SL-Track

The Hamilton LE comes with a sturdy SL-track that combines the benefits of traditional massage tracks. With its intelligent body scanning system, the Hamilton maps your shoulders and spine in order to provide an exceptional massage experience.

Zero Gravity
Reclining

Osaki’s 3 steps zero gravity reclining places you in the most desirable sleeping posture; this calming position decreases pressure on your spine and eases muscle tension.

Space Saving
Technology

To utilize minimal space, the Hamilton requires only 2 inches away from the nearest wall. Our space saving technology allows you to maximize floor space, without needing an entire room for a singular massage chair.

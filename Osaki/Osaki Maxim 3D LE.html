3D Massage Mechanism
The Maxim LE includes an industry-leading Three Dimensional Massage mechanism. This advanced massage system allows for a comprehensive intelligent massage to soothe stress along your shoulders, spine, lumbar, and glutes.
In order to encompass the entirety of your body, the Maxim includes a sturdy Steel SL-Track Frame. This track extends from your neck and shoulders to your thighs and glutes and acts as a guide for the 3D massage rollers.
Zero Gravity
Reclining
Weightless relaxation is now tenable with the Maxim LE, simply activate the Zero Gravity function to make use of this NASA-inspired technology. By positioning your legs at a higher elevation, you can negate the traditional strain of gravity on your body.
Space Saving
Technology
By simultaneously sliding the Maxim forward while reclining, the chair is able to achieve a full zero gravity recline while only needing two inches between itself and the wall.

Measuring Guide
  • Calculate the doorway width by subtracting 2 inches from the smallest inner width of the door frame. (1 inch from each side)
  • Please use a standard measuring tape to measure the doorway.
  • If the door cannot be fully opened, open it as much as possible and measure the width from the edge of the door hinge.
  • If there is a rounded curve on the wall, calculate the inner width based on the most protruding point of the wall.

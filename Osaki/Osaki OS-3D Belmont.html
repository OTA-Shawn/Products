3D Massage Mechanism
Experience a new dimension of luxury with Belmont’s three-dimensional massage rollers. When used in tandem with the chair’s SL-Track your body will finally know true relaxation.
Through the use of a scientifically-engineered SL-Track, the massage rollers can extend from the base of the skull to the very edge of your hamstrings.
Zero Gravity
Reclining
Rest in a state of complete relaxation with Belmont’s Zero Gravity Reclining. This two-stage NASA-inspired technology effortlessly positions your posture to literally ‘take the weight off your shoulders.’
Space Saving
Technology
With Belmont’s Space Saving Technology, you can position your chair up to 3 inches from the wall and still create a fully realized recline.

Full Body
Air Massage
Belmont incorporates 20 air cells throughout the chair to provide a precise air compression therapy for all your needs. Air massage can also be adjusted in three sections and five intensity levels.
4 Area Heating
Battle tension and stress across the spectrum with Belmont’s heat therapy. Belmont includes targeted heat along the back and calves, while having the ability to adjust its heat on three levels.

The Osaki OS-Pro Yamato has a modern design with slick line, a comfortable feel, and wide range of settings and capabilities. The Yamato can massage you from the neck area to the glutes and upper hamstrings with L-track roller. With the Yamato, you can choose from a variety of auto massage programs, get comfortable, and relax.

Body Scan Technology

Sensor in the back massage module scans the curvature of user’s body for the accurate massager movement.

L-Track Massage

The L-Track extends from your neck, all the way down past your lower back, and finally wrapping underneath the back of your thighs.

Air Bag Massage

There are multiple airbags located throughout the massage chair to offer a full body massage experience. Airbags are located at the shoulders, the arms, and the calf and foot areas.

2 Stages of Zero Gravity

When placed in Zero Gravity position, the weight of your back will be virtually supported by the backrest. In this position when the knees are brought above the chest will optimize your blood flow, in order to relax the body more. There are 2 stages of zero gravity.

Space Saving

The innovative design team has engineered a space saving technology that slides the chair forward as the chair reclines.

Foot Massage &Calf Kneading

Spinning foot massager provides foot soothing kneading massage. The airbags on calves give pressure side to side which provides kneading massage.

Extendable Footrest

The footrest will extend out up to 5.5” to comfortably fit a user’s various height.

Heat Therapy

The heat therapy is a great compliment to the roller and airbag massage. Heating the body’s temperature is known to increase blood circulation and loosen tense muscles.

Side Controller

Side Controller gives user more convenient access.

Bluetooth Speakers

Bluetooth speakers can connect to your phone to listen to music.

Zero Gravity Reclining
The 4D Atlas XL features a 1-Stage Zero Gravity recline, providing a weightless sensation during the massage. Users can choose their preferred recline position, either automatically or by manually adjusting the chair using the remote.
Space Saving Technology
The 4D Atlas XL can be placed 12 inches from the wall while still allowing a full recline.
Footrest Extension
The legrest extends up to 11 inches (Foot: 6”, Calf: 5”) , making it adjustable for users of varying heights, accommodating individuals up to 6'8".
48-Cell Full Body Air Massage
Featuring 48 strategically placed air cells throughout the chair, it delivers layered, progressive compression. This air compression massage promotes better circulation, relieves muscle tension, and enhances the overall relaxation experience.

Measuring Guide
  • Calculate the doorway width by subtracting 2 inches from the smallest inner width of the door frame. (1 inch from each side)
  • Please use a standard measuring tape to measure the doorway.
  • If the door cannot be fully opened, open it as much as possible and measure the width from the edge of the door hinge.
  • If there is a rounded curve on the wall, calculate the inner width based on the most protruding point of the wall.

    Osaki 3D Otamic LE is a 3D-adjustable massage chair with a 53 inch SL-track rolling system. Unlike other 3D Massage chairs, The Otamic LE is exclusively engineered with the latest massage chair features, which make it one of the smoothest, quietest, and most reliable massage experiences available. The chair also comes standard with Osaki’s tri-core processor for faster response, transition, and reliability. The Otamic LE also offers an automatic footrest extension with heating in the footrest included.

    The Otamic LE comes equipped with 5 levels of 3D Massage Roller Depth Adjustment. The 3D Technology gives you control over the overall intensity of the massage, by moving the rollers in and out from the back of the chair. Unlike most 3D Massage chairs on the market, the chair lets you adjust the 3D Roller depth at any point in the massage, whether you’re in an auto mode, or a manual mode.

    The Osaki 3D Otamic LE also comes standard with Osaki’s latest massage rolling track, which massages from your neck, all the way down to your glutes and upper hamstrings. This is called an “SL” rolling track, and it works with the computer body scan at the beginning of every session to give every user a massage that fits their body as long as they’re between 5’0-6’3.

    The stream-lined Airbag design in the Otamic LE uses less airbags, while still giving a full-body air compression massage. This also increases the longevity and reliability of the chair, making sure that you maximize the lifespan of your chair.

    This Chair comes equipped with 9 different preset programs including: Recover, Deep Tissue, Chinese, Midday Nap, Thai, Rejuvenate, Relax, Neck/Shoulder, and Strengthen. These auto programs combine the 5 different massage techniques available in the chair. These 5 massage styles are: Kneading, Tapping, Shiatsu, Clapping, and Rolling.

    Unlike most massage chairs, the Osaki Otamic LE comes with 2 different heating zones. There’s heat in the lower back area, and also heat in the enclosed footrest. This can be turned on/off in the main menu, or on the side controller.

    Side panel controller is designed that the user can easily control the chair without using a remote controller. The user can adjusting reclining position, Zero Gravity, Auto massage program, Heating ON/ OFF and USB connection.

    The Otamic LE is equipped with Bluetooth technology that allows the user to listen to their favorite music and phone calls on high quality speakers in the headrest.

    The Otamic LE also comes with 2 stages of zero gravity, giving the user a weightless feeling during the massage. These 2 stages give the user an option on how far back they want their chair to recline automatically, or you can adjust the chair manually in the remote.

    Along with the 2 stages of Zero-Gravity recline, the Otamic LE comes standard with space-saving technology, that allows you to place the chair within 3 inches of the wall, even while fully-reclined. The chair will move out first and then up while moving into Zero-Gravity.

    The Otamic LE has designed to focus on your calf muscles and release any built up tension through the long journeys and pressure we experience each day. On the upper section of the leg rest, the air bag massagers will rotate in a circular motion to add a new dimension to the calf massage.

    The Otamic LE has Extendable Footrest which can be extended up to 6 Inches to reach the desired comfortable position which fits a user’s various height. When the chair relines where the user feels the most comfortable, the footrest extends to customize the user’s height.

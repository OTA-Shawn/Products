3D MASSAGE MECHANISM
The AI 3D Envy features the latest generation of a robotic 3D massage system. This 3D technology allows the massage heads to move up and down, side to side, and in and out from the backrest, ensuring precise targeting of key areas of the back while applying consistent pressure from the shoulders to the lower back.
AI BODY CONDITION DETECTOR
With the latest advancements in AI, Osaki has integrated this cutting-edge technology into our chairs. By placing your fingers and palms on the sensor, the system detects where your tension points are concentrated by analyzing slight variations in your heart rate. Using this information, the AI system creates a personalized massage designed to maximize effectiveness based on your body's specific needs.
AUTOMATIC BODY SCAN
The automatic body scan customizes the massage for maximum comfort and effectiveness by detecting the user’s individual shape and size.
SPACE-SAVING TECHNOLOGY
The space-saving technology helps you make the most of your space. As the chair reclines, it glides forward, allowing it to fully recline even when placed just 3 inches from the wall.
2-STAGE ZERO GRAVITY
Inspired by NASA, the Osaki AI 3D Envy features Zero Gravity positioning, where your legs are elevated to heart level, reclining you to approximately 165 degrees. This position promotes even weight distribution, reducing pressure on the back and improving circulation in the legs. It also ensures consistent pressure from the roller heads, providing a balanced massage from the shoulders to the lower back.
SL-TRACK SYSTEM
Made out of from Laser-Crafted Steel, the Osaki Envy’s SL-track Technology allows the massage rollers to extend from your head to your hamstrings , delivering an incredibly smooth full body massage.

38-CELL FULL BODY AIR MASSAGE
The Osaki Envy is equipped with 38 air cells strategically placed throughout the chair, delivering a full-body massage that also promotes improved blood circulation.
2 SPECIALIZED FOOT ROLLERS
Dual foot rollers are located at the bottom of the footrest, rotating as nodes along the surface stimulate key areas of the foot. The nodes provide a sensation similar to thumbs pressing into the balls, arch, and heel, helping to stretch muscles and connective tissues. They are also designed to target key reflexology acupuncture points, promoting better circulation and relief.
AUTOMATIC EXTENDABLE
FOOTREST
The Osaki Envy can extend up to 6.5 inches, making it suitable for users of various heights. Its built-in body scan detects the user's leg length and automatically adjusts the footrest extension for a perfect fit.
LUMBAR & CALF HEAT THERAPY
Infrared heat pads are located in the lumbar and calf areas, emitting gentle heat to enhance the massage's effectiveness. The warmth helps loosen muscle tissue, allowing the air compression in the legs and the back rollers to work more quickly and deeply. Heat will also promote improved circulation. You can control the heat using the remote.
HAND REFLEXOLOGY
The Osaki Envy features mini nodes in the armrests for acupuncture hand reflexology, designed to alleviate arm fatigue and improve blood circulation.

Measuring Guide
  • Calculate the doorway width by subtracting 2 inches from the smallest inner width of the door frame. (1 inch from each side)
  • Please use a standard measuring tape to measure the doorway.
  • If the door cannot be fully opened, open it as much as possible and measure the width from the edge of the door hinge.
  • If there is a rounded curve on the wall, calculate the inner width based on the most protruding point of the wall.

The 4D Bravo Duo Mech is a state-of-the-art massage chair designed with advanced features and technology to deliver a personalized and soothing massage experience. It features a dual massage mechanism system, including upper rollers that cover the entire back and lower rollers targeting the glutes and upper hamstrings. It boasts a self-adaptive flexible SL-track that ensures optimal support and comfort.

SELF-ADAPTIVE FLEXIBLE SL-TRACK
The Osaki Bravo combines the flexible track system and the ability to extend while the chair is reclining. This makes it more effective in stretching the back and ensures a thorough massage experience while covering the neck, shoulders, entire length of the spine, and to the hips and thighs.
4D DUAL
MECHANISM™ MASSAGE SYSTEM
The Osaki Bravo features a 4D massage mechanism to target various areas of the body. The upper mechanism covers the back, providing a range of massage styles and intensities, while the lower mechanism delivers a consistent, penetrating massage to the glutes and upper hamstrings.
COMPUTER BODY SCAN
The Osaki Bravo features a Computer Body Scan that intelligently maps and adjusts the massage mechanism to the user's body contours. This technology ensures an optimal massage experience by tailoring its movements and intensity to each individual user.
INTELLIGENT VOICE CONTROL
The Osaki Bravo massage chair's voice control capabilities offer convenience and ease of use. For a smooth and enjoyable experience, be sure to follow the guidelines and commands outlined in the manual.
38-CELL FULL BODY AIR MASSAGE
The Osaki Bravo includes 38 air cells that targets multiple areas, such as the shoulders, arms, calves, and feet. By applying consistent, gentle pressure to these areas, air massages can enhance circulation and relieve muscle tension.

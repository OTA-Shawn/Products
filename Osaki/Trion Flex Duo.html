The Trion Flex Duo features an integrated health monitor that tracks heart rate, oxygen levels, microcirculation, and fatigue. Its AI technology uses this data to deliver a personalized massage tailored to your body’s needs.
INTELLIGENT VOICE COMMAND
The Trion Flex Duo features built-in voice control for a truly hands-free experience. Just say "Hey Alice" followed by a preset command, and experience an uninterrupted hands-free massage.
RELAXING 3 ARM Rollers
Each side of the chair features 3 active arm rollers that provide a soothing massage to relieve tension and boost blood circulation. This rolling action works in harmony with the air compression massage, enhancing both your relaxation and recovery experience.
4D MASSAGE MECHANISM
The Trion Flex Duo is a 4D massage chair that replicates the feel of a real human masseuse using advanced 3D technology that moves vertically, horizontally, and in-depth for intensity, while the fourth dimension adds variations in speed and rhythm.

Measuring Guide
  • Calculate the doorway width by subtracting 2 inches from the smallest inner width of the door frame. (1 inch from each side)
  • Please use a standard measuring tape to measure the doorway.
  • If the door cannot be fully opened, open it as much as possible and measure the width from the edge of the door hinge.
  • If there is a rounded curve on the wall, calculate the inner width based on the most protruding point of the wall.

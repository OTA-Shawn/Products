The OS-4000T massage chair features beautifully updated upholstery and includes Zero Gravity, a 30 inch Roller Stroke, Body Scan Technology, S-Track Massage Robot, full body air massage, seat vibration, and heat therapy for your back.

2D Massage Mechanism
The advanced 2D Massage expands beyond the typical constraints of other massage chairs. By moving on multiple axises, the 4000T massage rollers give an intensive massage experience.
Intelligent
Body Scanning
Equipped with the latest technology, the rollers to map the back curvature with even more accuracy, which translates into a more precise massage hitting all the key areas. Regardless of your body type, this highly advanced chair will self-adjust making each massage experience feel customized.
S-track System
The S-track system allows the roller heads to massage along the back from the neck down to the lower spine with a consistent pressure in the massage, along with a comfortable support.
3-stage Zero Gravity
With the 4000T’s three-stage zero gravity reclining, you can enjoy a complete state of relaxation. This NASA-inspired technology adjusts your position to let your body have a lightweight experience.

Heat Therapy
The heat therapy is a great compliment to roller and airbag massage. The heating pad is not intended to be use for more than 30 minutes a day or the duration of a massage preset program.

Automatic Extendable Footrest
The calf and foot massager is capable of making adjustments according to each individual's leg length, ensuring that the massage air bags are concentrating on the correct areas.
Convenient Remote Tablet
The 4000T includes a remote tablet for the user’s convenience to command the massage chair.
Located on the bottom of the feet are 3 spinning reflexology massagers on each side, providing a soothing kneading style application. As the rollers spin it will stimulate acupuncture points and as the air bags inflate it will generate a much deeper massage along the bottoms of your feet.
Vibrating Seat
Located in the seat base is a vibration plate that provides a vibration massage to the back of the thighs.
Wireless Controller
Located on the top right of the main remote you will find a compact wireless remote allowing you to control the main features of the chair without having to struggle to reach the main remote in the reclined position.

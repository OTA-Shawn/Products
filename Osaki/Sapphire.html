With the Osaki Sapphire, you are experiencing premium luxury enhanced by some of the most advanced technologies and innovations in the industry. From top to bottom, every detail is engineered to deliver a complete, full-body massage experience.

While most massage chairs rely solely on simple airbag compression that inflates and deflates, the Osaki Sapphire goes far beyond. It features true kneading mechanisms across the forearms, wrists, and hands areas that are constantly in use and often overloaded with tension. This targeted kneading helps loosen tight muscles and tendons, providing a deeper and more therapeutic level of relief.
Calf & Foot rollers
The Sapphire 4D+ rollers in the calf and foot take leg massage to the next level, delivering a kneading motion that target key pressure points and help ease tension.
Adjustable shoulders
The Sapphire 4D+ features shoulder airbags with up to three adjustable width settings , allowing the chair to accommodate users of varying body types with a more customized fit.
BODY SCAN TECHNOLOGY
Automatic body scan technology maps your body’s shape and contours to deliver a personalized massage, ensuring precise roller placement and maximum comfort for the most effective, relaxing experience.
3-stage zero Gravity Reclining
Feel the sensation of weightlessness in the Sapphire 4D+ 3-Stage Zero Gravity. As you recline, your weight is evenly distributed, relieving pressure on your back and elevating comfort for an immersive massage experience. The Sapphire 4D+’s Zero Gravity position allows more space between the chair and the wall, as it glides forward instead of reclining backward.
SL-Track SYSTEM
The extended SL track merges the benefits of both S and L-shaped tracks, allowing the massage rollers to follow the natural curve of your spine while extending from your neck down to your hamstrings. This design ensures more comprehensive coverage of your back and legs, targeting key muscle groups for deeper relaxation.
46-CELL
AIR Compression MASSAGE
Equipped with 46 strategically placed air cells, the Sapphire 4D+ gently eases tension across the body. Each session provides targeted compression therapy to boost relaxation and support overall well-being.

HEATING therapy
The Sapphire 4D+ is equipped with soothing heating therapy in the armrest and calves, effectively help warming and releasing tension in those areas. The top massage rollers feature soothing heat for a deeper, more therapeutic massage.

The Ai Vivo 4D+2D is a cutting-edge massage chair that incorporates advanced features and technology to provide a customized and relaxing massage experience. It is equipped with a dual massage mechanism system, which consists of 4D rollers at the entire back and 2D rollers at the bottom of the track, covering the glutes and top of the hamstring. This highly advanced massage system is driven by its Ai technology that tailors the most optimal massage based on your body’s condition.

Whether you're looking for a deep tissue massage or a gentle relaxation session, the Vivo massage chair is designed to deliver a soothing and therapeutic experience.

Technology
The chair utilizes the built-in health monitor and gathers data for the Ai driven system to detect tension and stress in your torso area. By analyzing this data, it can create a tailored massage program to your specific needs and preferences.
Intelligent Health Detection
The Osaki Ai Vivo 4D+2D massage chair has advanced capabilities with its built-in health monitor. By detecting various metrics such as heart rate, blood oxygen levels, microcirculation, and fatigue index, the chair's Ai technology can provide a personalized massage experience tailored to your body's current condition.
Three Arm Rollers
When activated, the arm rollers gently knead and apply pressure to the muscles, tendons, and pressure points in the arms. This creates a soothing and invigorating sensation, effectively reducing tension and promoting better blood circulation.
Self-Adaptive Flexible SL-track
The Osaki Ai Vivo combines the flexible track system and the ability to extend while the chair is reclining. This enhances the massage chair's effectiveness of stretching the back and ensures a more comprehensive massage experience while providing coverage along the neck, shoulders, entire length of the spine, and to the hips and thighs.
Duo Mech™
4D + 2D Massage Mechanism Technology
The Osaki Ai Vivo massage chair incorporates both a 4D and 2D massage mechanism to target different areas of the body. The back is covered by the 4D mechanism delivering a variety of massage styles and intensities, while the bottom part delivers a consistent penetrating massage to the glutes and the top of the hamstring.
Intelligent Voice Control
Having voice control capabilities in the Osaki Ai Vivo massage chair is convenient and user-friendly. Make sure to follow the guidelines and commands mentioned in the manual to ensure a smooth and enjoyable experience with our Ai Vivo massage chair.
Kneading & Rubbing Legrest
The Osaki Ai Vivo features a 360-degree rotating kneading and rubbing massages for the calves. In addition to the rotating massages, the chair also utilizes air compression to enhance blood circulation and provide muscle relief.

The Ai Vivo 4D+2D is a cutting-edge massage chair that incorporates advanced features and technology to provide a customized and relaxing massage experience. It is equipped with a dual massage mechanism system, which consists of 4D rollers at the entire back and 2D rollers at the bottom of the track, covering the glutes and top of the hamstring. This highly advanced massage system is driven by its Ai technology that tailors the most optimal massage based on your body’s condition.

Whether you're looking for a deep tissue massage or a gentle relaxation session, the Vivo massage chair is designed to deliver a soothing and therapeutic experience.

The Osaki Ai Vivo conbines the flexible track system and the ability to extend while the chair is reclining.

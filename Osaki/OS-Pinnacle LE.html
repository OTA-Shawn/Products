The Pinnacle LE massage chair seamlessly complements modern interior trends with its clean, simple design. It offers an unparalleled relaxation experience, equipped with cutting-edge features and technology. Enjoy the benefits of a soothing massage in the comfort and privacy of your own home or office.
Zero Gravity Reclining & Space Saving Design
Inspired by NASA technology, the Pinnacle LE offers zero gravity positioning, reclining the body and elevating the legs to evenly distribute weight and reduce pressure on the back. This promotes better blood circulation and nhances overall comfort.
2D Massage Mechanism
The 2D massage mechanism moves along both up/down and left/right axes, targeting key areas of the back. The massage heads knead and tap, effectively loosening muscle tension for enhanced relief.

Full Body Air Massage
The Pinnacle LE features 22 airbags strategically placed around the shoulders, arms, calves, and feet, providing a comprehensive and full-body massage experience.
Heating Therapy
The lower back and calves are equipped with infrared heating, which gently applies mild warmth to enhance the massage. This mild heat helps relax muscles more quickly and promotes better circulation.
Dual Foot Rollers
Dual foot rollers at the base of the footrest rotate to massage key reflexology points while stretching and relieving tension in the tendons.
Auto Extension Footrest
The leg rest can be extended up to 8 inches using the remote control, accommodating users of different heights and fitting individuals up to 6'2" tall.
Tablet Controler
The remote features a full touchscreen interface that is easy to use and navigate, allowing you to customize your massage or select from a variety of preset programs with ease.
Quick Access Control Panel
The Pinnacle LE is designed with the customer in mind, featuring quick controls conveniently located on the top of the side panel. With just a simple press of a button, you can easily position the massage or start your session.

Measuring Guide
  • Calculate the doorway width by subtracting 2 inches from the smallest inner width of the door frame. (1 inch from each side)
  • Please use a standard measuring tape to measure the doorway.
  • If the door cannot be fully opened, open it as much as possible and measure the width from the edge of the door hinge.
  • If there is a rounded curve on the wall, calculate the inner width based on the most protruding point of the wall.

The Sonoma 4D massage chair offers a luxurious, full-body massage with advanced 4D technology. It features adjustable intensity, body scanning, heat therapy, zero gravity reclining, and pre-programmed massages targeting specific muscle groups. Combining innovative technology with ergonomic design, the Sonoma 4D provides optimal relaxation and therapeutic benefits.

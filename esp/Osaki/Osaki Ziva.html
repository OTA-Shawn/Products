2D MASSAGE MECHANISM
A 2D massage mechanism delivers a soothing massage by moving rollers along the back in two dimensions: up and down, and side to side. It effectively mimics human hands to provide kneading, tapping, and rolling motions, offering a therapeutic experience.
24-CELL FULL BODY AIR MASSAGE
A 24 air cell compression massage system uses strategically placed airbags to deliver a soothing and effective massage. These airbags inflate and deflate to apply gentle pressure to various parts of the body, help enhancing circulation.
BRUSHLESS MOTOR MASSAGE
A brushless motor massage provides quiet, efficient, and durable performance, enhancing the quality and longevity of the massage experience.
AUTOMATIC
BODY SCAN
The Osaki Ziva takes advantage of body scan technology to map the key areas of the neck and back, ensuring that there is optimal pressure applied during the massage. This body scan will detect your shoulder height and can be modified after the scan is completed.

SL-TRACK SYSTEM
Constructed from Laser-Crafted Steel, the Osaki Ziva’s SL-track feature extends the massage rollers from your your head to your glutes, providing a consistent relief for the entire back and lower body.
TOUCH TABLET CONTROLLER
The Osaki Ziva includes a touch tablet controller, enabling users to easily navigate and adjust their massage experience, such as position of the chair and massage programs.
ADJUSTABLE
LUMBAR HEATING
The Osaki Ziva provides heating therapy in the lumbar area, making a complimentary feature to add to the relaxation as it helps to relax any muscle tension.
ZERO GRAVITY TECHNOLOGY
With technology originally pioneered by NASA, Zero Gravity technology reclines the chair to position the user in a way that reduces pressure on the spine, enhancing relaxation and the effectiveness of the massage.
SPACE-SAVING TECHNOLOGY
With Osaki Ziva’s Space Saving Technology, the massage chair can be placed 4 inches away from the wall and still transition into a full reclining position.
SPRING-LOADED EXTENDABLE
FOOTREST
The Osaki Ziva can extend up to 7 inches, which can be adjusted by applying pressure with your feet. This features ensures that the chair can be accommodated to users of different body proportions.

REMOVABLE HEADREST

MINIMUM DOORWAY
Measuring Guide
  • Calculate the doorway width by subtracting 2 inches from the smallest inner width of the door frame. (1 inch from each side)
  • Please use a standard measuring tape to measure the doorway.
  • If the door cannot be fully opened, open it as much as possible and measure the width from the edge of the door hinge.
  • If there is a rounded curve on the wall, calculate the inner width based on the most protruding point of the wall.

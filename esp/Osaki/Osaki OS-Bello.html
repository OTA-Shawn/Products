Osaki OS-Bello is ergonomically designed to contour a variety of spine shape. This elegant designed chair features an L-track rolling massage system and 8 full body airbags. The design of Bello is stylish and chic to lend itself to a variety of decors while taking up a minimum amount of space.
L-Track Massage
The L-track rolling system allows the roller heads to massage neck down to through the glutes to the upper portion of the hamstrings. The massage roller system delivers a consistent amount of pressure as it rolls along the curves the back.
Zero Gravity & Cradle
OS-Bello features zero-gravity and cradle action. Inspired by NASA technology, the Zero Gravity position is the perfect position to enjoy a massage evenly distributing weight reducing pressure points in the back. Bello swings action moves upward and backward like a cradle to enhance your massage experience.
Space Saving
OS-Bello only requires about 3 inches of wall space from the wall allowing it to take small footprint in the room. When reclining the chair will glide forward to compensate for the reclining backrest.
Heating
OS-Bello features a heating mode on the back area which can be manually controlled on the side mounted remote. Heating therapy is a great compliment to the massage rollers and airbag massage. Heating the bodyâ€™s temperature is known to increase blood circulation and loosen tense muscles.
Airbags
There are 8 airbags located throughout the OS-Bello to offer a full body massage experience. Airbags are located at the shoulders, the arms, the thigh, and the calf areas. There are 3 levels of air pressure intensity that the user can manage.
Acu-pressure massage on the Calves
As the calf airbags inflate there pressure nodes that will press into the back of the calves hitting a pressure point to stretch and loosen muscles
Automatic Footrest Recline
Footrest automatically moves when moving to the reclining position. The footrest angle can be independently adjusted from the recline angle
Side Controller
OS-Bello controller is located above the right arm area which the user can easily access to control the massage chair. On the remote it allows the user to select the massage type, intensity, speed, program and location. You can also operate the functions independently in the Manual Mode. The user can customize their massage by pressing the Spot or Partial Back Massage button which allows the user to localize the massage.
Bluetooth Speaker
OS-Bello is equipped with Bluetooth technology that allows the user to listen music on high quality speakers which is located at the headrest. Enhance your massage experience while listening to your favorite tunes or relaxing sounds..
Retractable Hanger
OS-Bello features a retractable hanger on the side of the chair for convenience. The user can hang clothes, bags or other personal items during the massage..
Removable Cushions
Back cushion can be removed by the zipper at the top of the backrest. The cushion on the additional seat cushion is easily removed as well.

    Introducing the ALL NEW luxurious AmaMedic Hilux 4D massage chair, designed with all the newest technology, luxury, and stylish elegance. Even the smallest of details have been carefully inspected and crafted with the highest quality.

    The newly enhanced 4D L-track massage is designed to massage wider, deeper, and more precisely than the conventional massage rollers. The 4D massage technology will mimic the hands of a professional masseur. The AmaMedic Hilux 4D offers 8 different levels of 4D intensity. With the variety of adjustment levels, such as 5 levels of air massage intensity and speed of the rollers.

    The AmaMedic Hilux 4D has 8 automatic massage programs to choose from.
    Demo : This program will cycle through briefly all its different massages it has to offer.
    Relax : This program utilizes a softer massage to help bring the user into a more relax state where the massage is less aggressive.
    Swing : This program will massage the user while rocking in a back and forth movement to create a more calming state of mind and experience.
    Stretch : This program will inflate certain regions pinning you to the chair and then proceed to stretch out the body from your legs to your shoulder while getting massaged
    Scraping : This program perform a scraping style massage pressing in and releasing in a down and upward motion. This is great to stretch out muscles in isolated areas.
    Shiatsu : This program helps to perform a more pinpointed massage along the back. This massage style helps to focus on certain pressure points in the back targeting tense muscles.
    Gentle Relief : This program helps to performs a tapping action with tapping.
    Rejuvenate : This program utilizes the strong massage styles and aggressively massages the back.

    The AmaMedic Hilux 4D has the most advanced back heating system with a mild infrared heat. The roller itself will heat up at the same time. The heated back roller allows for a far greater surface heating area, From the neck down to the hamstrings. It applies an infrared heat which is deep penetrating compared to traditional thermal heat with the high temperatures. The warm heat will compliment the massage in loosening the muscles.

    The Hilux combines both the S and L track technology for an exceptional massage experience. The S-Track rollers will contour the natural curvature of your spine. The L-Track extends from your neck, all the way down past your lower back, and finally massaging underneath the back of your upper thighs. The L-Track offers about 50% more massage area than your average S-Track chairs.

    There are multiple airbags located throughout the massage chair to offer a full body massage experience. The Hilux offers a total of 18 highly advanced airbags in the massage chair. Airbags are located at the shoulders, arms, waist and pelvis, and the calf areas. The total air bags have been reduced but covers more surface area than previous technology with far greater efficiency and reliability.

    Regardless of body type the adjustable shoulder massager can be easily tightened by pushing the massagers inwards. There are 3 adjustable sizes on each side. To reset to the default width simply push in all the way on both sides and it will release.

    Side panel controller is designed that the user can easily control the chair without using a remote tablet. The user can adjusting 4D intensity, reclining positions, Zero Gravity, Auto massage program and Heating function.

    USB Charger & Wireless Charger
    AmaMedic Hilux 4D thoughtfully designed with a wireless charger for the modern times of digital devices. The user can charge their smartphone on the wireless charging pad located at the top of the armrest. Please not the wireless charger is only available with wireless charging capability.

    Touch Screen Controller
    The AmaMedic Hilux 4D’s remote is upgraded to Touch Screen Remote Tablet. It has an easy view screen displaying the function of the chair. It also has magnetic holder that holds the remote into place. The interface is very easy to navigate and adjust settings.

    Hilux is equipped with 2 stages of zero gravity, giving the user a weightless feeling during the massage. This gives the user an option on how far back they want their chair to recline automatically, or you can adjust the chair manually with the remote. Inspired by NASA the Zero Gravity is to help reduce the stress on the back, evenly distributing the weight and pressure.

    The Hilux 4D only needs 4.7” from the backrest to the wall, which reduces the amount of space the chair takes up. Space saving design allows you to maximize your valuable living space.

    On the upper section of the leg rest, the air massagers will rotate in a circular motion to add a new dimension to the calf massage. For the foot portion addition components have been added to stretch and massage the bridge and heel of the foot. The experience is like no other.

    Underneath the feet there are spinning reflexology rollers to provide an outstanding foot massage. The rollers under the feet will travel along the sides and arches of your feet to provide a more dimensional kneading experience.

    The foot component will extend outwards and then retract inwards when scanning the length of the user’s legs. Simply apply pressure with your feet to indicated to the leg scanner that length is at the desired position. remote also allows for the user to auto adjust the legrest to either extend or retract.

    Hilux 4D is equipped with Bluetooth technology with an Amphitheater sound system that reduces the surrounding noise, enhancing your listening experience. This allows you to listen to your favorite tunes or podcast on AmaMedic’s highest grade of audio speakers located in the headrest. It is advised to activate the Bluetooth speakers at the lowest volume before playing music, and then gradually increasing to a desired level.

    The Hilux 4D has 20 different auto massage programs divided into 4 different massage area categories. There is Neck & Shoulder, Lower Back, Manual and Advanced categories.

One of the most often overlooked portions of the body during massage therapy is your feet. After a long day of standing and running around, your feet need to be taken care of properly for longevity’s sake. Amamedic created the Shiatsu Foot Massager to aid in this vital recovery process.

Heated Massage

The Shiatsu Foot Massager employs a deep tissue foot massage in tandem with soothing mild heat in order to provide the most comprehensive and thorough foot massage on the market.

Intuitive Control

Easily control the device without having to bend over with the intuitive one-button design. Click once to power on, tap a second time to enable the mild heating functions, and a third time to power down.

Ergonomic Shape

Unlike most other foot massagers, the Shiatsu Foot Massager was designed from the sole down. Intentionally crafted to match and compliment the contour of the foot, this massager’s ergonomic design works around, not against, your foot’s natural curvature.

Usage on Lower Back

Whereas most massagers are intended solely for use in one part of the body, the Shiatsu Foot Massager may be utilized on the lower back as well. It’s lightweight design and new-age base grips confidently to most chairs, allowing for a quality deep tissue massage on both your lumbar and feet.

Usage on Calves

In addition to being utilized on the back and feet, you may find relief by using this device on your calves as well. Simply prop your leg up and rest it upon the massage heads to receive an instant deep tissue massage upon your calf.

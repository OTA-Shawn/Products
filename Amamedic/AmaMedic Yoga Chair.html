Adjustable Positions
This device isn’t a one-trick pony nor a one-hit wonder; instead, the Yoga Chair manages to succeed in a variety of functions all without overcomplicating the process. Set the chair into one of the following positions to fully utilize the desired effects.
1. Lumbar Stretch: This overall massage is fantastic for everyday use, covering all aspects of the body with varying degrees of pressure and power.
2. Neutral Relaxation: Neutral relaxation allows the body and mind to enter a state of deep relaxation, which helps reduce stress levels and calm the nervous system.
3. Spinal Contraction: This gentler program focuses on the upper back, arms, calves, and feet with mild compression and awareness.
4. Ankle & Knee Stretch: As the chair rocks you back and forth between the zero gravity positions, it also stretches out the legs while providing gentle pressure throughout the body.
5. Posture-Correcting Lounge: Focuses on fixing posture for the best alignment while sitting a relaxing sitting position.
6. Hamstring Stretch: Use this massage when your battery is low to get a quick boost of energy, achieve this through a series of slow deliberate kneading and tapping processes.
7. Enhanced Leg Blood Flow: By elevating your legs above your heart, achieve a healthier state of blood flow than normally experienced.
8. Enhanced Body Blood Flow: Invert the traditional state of blood flow by resting your shoulders and head below your heart and your legs above it. Used briefly, this position will improve blood flow across the entire body.
9. Spinal Extension: Stretch out the upper body and spine by extending the vertebrae as opposed to the normal contraction.
Additionally, the chair is fully customizable, allowing you to experiment with different positions and combinations to achieve the best results for your particular situation.
There’s no need to overcomplicate the process, so the Amamedic Yoga Chair includes an intuitive remote control with four handy buttons. The top button allows you to independently control the back of the chair, the next controls the lumbar/glute section of the chair, and the third controls the legs independently. Lastly, holding the HOME button returns the chair back to the inverted ‘V’ position.
Once you’ve achieved the desired position you may holster the remote along the side of the chair for quick & convenient access whenever you’re ready to reposition again.

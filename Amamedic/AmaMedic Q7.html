AmaMedic Q7 is a Deluxe Massage Chair with 8 fixed massage rollers from the neck to the upper waist. The Q7 is designed as a compact and deluxe sized massage chair with is suitable for small to medium body shaped user. The chair comes equipped with 2 stages of Zero Gravity, air massage with 16 airbags, heating on the lumbar, foot roller massage with 6 automatic massage programs.

The Q7 is designed with 8 fixed massage rollers from the neck down to the lower back areas, which can effectively help to relief muscle tensions and back pains.

Inspired by NASA technology, the Zero gravity position is the perfect position to enjoy a massage, aligning your back relative to your thigh at an optimal level of comfort. When reclined into the zero-gravity position, the complete weight of your back is virtually supported by the backrest which maximizes the intensity of the massage.
AmaMedic Q7 is equipped with 2 stages of Zero Gravity, giving the user a weightless feeling during the massage. This gives the user an option on how far back they want their chair to recline automatically, or you can adjust the chair manually with the remote.

The AmaMedic Q7 is equipped with 6 different preset programs including: Refresh, Relax, Yoga, Neck & Shoulder, Waist & Back and Dream.
Refresh: Master mode of full body massage, and it offers the strong intensity of roller and airbag massage.
Relax: Full Body Relaxing mode with the medium intensity of roller and airbag massage.
Yoga: Full body yoga program with gentle and smooth stretching.
Neck & Shoulder: Partial massage which focusing on the neck and shoulder area to help muscle strain with the roller and airbag massages.
Waist & Back: Partial massage on the waist and back area to help to improve back pain with the roller massage focusing on the waist and back areas.
Dream: Full Body Massage with the light intensity of roller.

Side shortcut controller is designed that the user can easily control the chair without using a remote controller. The user can adjusting reclining position, Zero Gravity, Auto massage program, Airbags, Rollers, Power ON/ OFF and USB connection.

AmaMedic Q7 is equipped with Bluetooth technology, enhancing your listening experience. This allows you to listen to your favorite music on Osaki's highest grade of audio speakers located in the headrest. It is advised to activate the Bluetooth speakers at the lowest volume before playing music, and then gradually increasing to a desired level. The blue light on the speakers will indicate if the speakers have synced with your device.

There are multiple airbags located throughout the massage chair to offer a full body massage experience. The Q7 offers a total of 22 airbags in the massage. Airbags are located at the shoulders, arms, waist and pelvis, and the calf areas. This compression massage helps to continue increasing blood circulation throughout the body to relieve pain and tension.

Q7 has heating pads located on the Lumbar area. It offers heat therapy which is a great compliment to the roller and airbag massage. Heating the body's temperature is known to increase blood circulation and loosen tense muscles. This can be turned on/off in the main menu, or on the side controller.

On the bottom of the foot massager rollers that spin along the arch of the feet which is complimented by airbag massage. As the airbags inflate the feet are pushed down on the spinning rollers increasing the intensity.

AmaMedic Q7 has an Extendable Footrest which can be extended up to 6 inches allowing to accommodate various heights. When initiating the massage, the leg rest will extend completely out and then begin to retract. By firmly pressing down with your feet when reaching the desired length, it will indicate to stop retracting and start the massage.

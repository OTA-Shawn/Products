AmaMedic Q7 is a deluxe massage chair with 8 fixed rollers for neck to upper waist. Compact and deluxe-sized, suitable for small to medium body. Features 2 stages of Zero Gravity, 16 airbags for air massage, lumbar heating, and foot roller massage with 6 auto programs.

8 Fixed Massage Rollers
The Q7 is designed with 8 fixed massage rollers from the neck down to the lower back areas, which can effectively help to relief muscle tensions and back pains.
2 Steps Zero Gravity
AmaMedic Q7 offers 2 stages of Zero Gravity for weightless massage. Back fully supported by backrest, maximizing intensity. Choose auto recline or manual with remote.

Q7 has 22 airbags for a full-body massage, targeting shoulders, arms, waist, pelvis, and calves. Compression massage improves blood circulation, relieving pain and tension.

Surround Sound Bluetooth Speaker
AmaMedic Q7 has Bluetooth tech for music. High-grade speakers in headrest. Start at low volume, then increase. Blue light shows sync with device.

Foot Rollers
Bottom foot massager rollers spin along feet arch, complemented by airbag massage. Inflating airbags push feet down on rollers, increasing intensity.
Extendable Footrest
AmaMedic Q7's Extendable Footrest extends up to 6 inches for height adjustments. During massage start, the leg rest fully extends; press firmly with feet to begin.

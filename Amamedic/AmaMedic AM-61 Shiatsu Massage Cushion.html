The new Amamedic Shiatsu Massage Cushion is the massage chair of the modern day magnate. Compact and lightweight, the Shiatsu Massage Cushion can be used anywhere, whether you’re stuck at your 9-5 or crossing the Pacific on flight 95.
Deep Tissue Shiatsu It should come as no surprise that the Shiatsu Massage Cushion specializes in thorough shiatsu massages. This cushion is equipped with Amamedic’s patented massage technology, providing a truly unique massage experience.
Use Anywhere While most portable massagers are designed for specialized local use, the Amamedic Shiatsu Massage Cushion was crafted to be used across the entire body. While sitting or lying down comfortably, the cushion may be used on the lumbar, shoulders, and neck; additionally, the shiatsu rollers can be placed directly against problem areas along the arms and legs for instant relief.
Heat Massage Function Unlike traditional portable massagers, the Shiatsu Cushion includes a luxury heated massage function. Intuitive and user friendly, this function can be used on the lower back to provide mild lumbar stress relief. In the Winter, use this function for a cozy escape from the cold.
Four Massage Heads Whereas most consumer massagers come equipped with a mere two massage heads, the Shiatsu Massage Cushion utilizes four individualized massage heads for a deep and thorough massage experience.
15 Minute Timer
To save power, the Massage Cushion is intelligently programmed to automatically shut off after 15 minutes of use; allowing the user to safely and comfortably fall asleep during the massage session without concern.
Easy Use The easy to use design allows for both intuitive control and an aesthetically appealing design. The two button system can be used to power the massager on & off, change the direction of the roller heads, and activate the heated function.

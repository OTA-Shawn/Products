Universal Usage The lightweight and compact design of this Neck Massager means that it can travel with you wherever you go. Keep it at your desk, in your nightstand, in your carry-on, or even next to the couch! All without taking up a cumbersome amount of your limited space.
15 Minute Auto Timer Feel safe and confident falling asleep with your Amamedic Neck Massager, as it comes equipped with an intelligent shut off timer. After 15 minutes of continuous use, the shiatsu rollers will automatically power down, saving energy and providing the right amount of time of soothing massage.
Breathable Memory Foam
The Shiatsu Neck Massager utilizes high quality memory foam that regulates temperature, yielding a comfortable and breathable material that you can get lost in. This cloud-like material both supports the neck while capitulating to portions of the head that rest upon it.
Heat Therapy To assist with the deep tissue shiatsu massage, the Amamedic Neck Massager employs mild heated rollers for the optimal massage experience. You can easily control the use of heat with the intuitive click of the power button.
Dual Massage Modes
Control your massage by simply clicking the power button. Press once to turn the pillow on, cycle through kneading, steering and mild heated massages via subsequent presses, and power the massager off by pressing one final time.
